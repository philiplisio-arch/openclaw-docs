# AI Brain Project Roadmap (Draft v0.2)

## Guiding Principle

The AI Brain is not a single application. It is a long-term operating system for Philip's work, projects, knowledge, and decision-making.

The primary interface is conversation. Technology exists to support the conversation, not replace it.

---

# Phase 0 — Discovery (Understand Before Designing)

## 0.1 Intentions

Define what success looks like.

Questions include:

- What should the AI Brain become over the next 5–10 years?
- What problems should it solve?
- What relationship should Philip have with it?
- Which decisions remain human decisions?

Deliverable:
- AI Brain Charter

---

## 0.3 Architectural Archaeology

Understand how today's system evolved.

For every major component ask:

- Why did it appear?
- What problem did it solve?
- Why did Philip continue using it?
- What frustrations exist?
- What unexpected strengths emerged?

Goal:

Capture the lessons learned from the past year of experimentation rather than starting from a blank sheet.

Deliverable:
- Evolution of the AI Ecosystem

# Design Principles

- Conversation is the primary interface.
- Memory is a strategic asset and must be intentionally designed.
- Evidence and documented decisions take precedence over vague recollection.
- The AI Brain supports multiple concurrent projects within one shared operating environment.
- The architecture must evolve as AI capabilities advance.
- Favor modular components that can be replaced without redesigning the whole system.
