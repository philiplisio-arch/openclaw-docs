[Securities Times, 2026-06-30](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3989020.html).
- Deerfield Management (a global healthcare investment platform) has over **$16 billion** in assets under management and has invested in more than 200 healthcare companies, according to Deerfield Management. [Securities Times, 2026-06-30](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3989020.html).

**Cambricon  becomes first trillion-yuan market cap stock on STAR Market, up 363%** · covered by 3 outlets

- From the low of 348 yuan in July 2025, the stock price rose **363 %** in eleven months. [Jiemian, 2026-07-01](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14671166.html).
- In 2025, Cambricon achieved revenue of **6.497 billion yuan** and net profit of **2.059 billion yuan**, ending years of losses. [Jiemian, 2026-07-01](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14671166.html).
- Goldman Sachs raised its net profit forecast for Cambricon for 2026–2030 by **30 % to 68 %** and raised its target price to **2,406 yuan**, according to Goldman Sachs. [Jiemian, 2026-07-01](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14671166.html).

### Policy

**SAMR  launches special action to boost antitrust enforcement in mergers** · covered by 2 outlets
- The State Administration for Market Regulation (SAMR) issued a notice to deploy a special action from June to December to improve the efficiency of anti-monopoly review of business mergers, aiming to support enterprises in overcoming ‘involution-style’ competition through mergers and acquisitions, according to SAMR. [People’s Daily, 2026-06-30](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0630/c1004-40751089.html).
- The action includes three key tasks: strengthening regulatory capacity, improving full-chain supervision quality, and enhancing compliance guidance, and will explore green channels for review in specific industries, according to SAMR. [People’s Daily, 2026-06-30](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0630/c1004-40751089.html).
- The ‘double twenty’ time limit for simple cases (20 days for initial review and 20 days for further review) will continue to be strictly enforced, according to SAMR. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-30](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260630/herald/0d02d52e010db10c0a1c4905f57a0505.html).

**PBOC  conducts first overnight reverse repo to ease short-term liquidity** · covered by 7 outlets
- The People’s Bank of China (PBOC) conducted its first-ever overnight reverse repo operation on June 29, totaling **300 billion yuan**, using a ‘fixed rate, quantity-based bidding’ mechanism, according to the PBOC. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-06-30](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-06-30/doc-inifcewq6879688.shtml).
- On June 30, the PBOC conducted a **600 billion yuan** overnight reverse repo operation alongside a **69.5 billion yuan** 7-day reverse repo at a rate of 1.40 %, according to the PBOC. [Securities Times, 2026-06-30](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3988012.html).
- PBoC Governor Pan Gongsheng  stated on June 17 that the central bank would enrich the open market operations toolkit and add an overnight reverse repo instrument, according to the PBOC. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-06-30](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-06-30/doc-inifcewq6879688.shtml).

### Macro

**China’s manufacturing PMI returns to expansion in June** · covered by 8 outlets
- Manufacturing PMI rose to **50.3 %** in June, up 0.3 percentage points from the previous month, entering expansion territory, according to the National Bureau of Statistics . [People’s Daily, 2026-06-30](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0630/c1004-40750737.html).
- High-tech manufacturing PMI reached **53.5 %** in June, up 0.6 percentage points, rising for four consecutive months, according to the China Logistics Information Center. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-30](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260630/herald/a0bf6d8edfdf2e4699ca7d7f7d9f30b3.html).
- The services business activity index was **50.4 %** in June, up 0.1 percentage point, while the construction business activity index was **49.0 %**, still below 50, according to the NBS. [Securities Times, 2026-06-30](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3988057.html).

**China’s state-owned enterprises profit up 3.5% in Jan-May** · covered by 4 outlets
- In the first five months, total profits of state-owned enterprises reached **1,725.80 billion yuan**, up **3.5 %** year-on-year, while total operating revenue was **32,822.16 billion yuan**, down **0.7 %** year-on-year, according to the Ministry of Finance . [People’s Daily, 2026-06-30](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0630/c1004-40751064.html).
- As of the end of May, the asset-liability ratio of state-owned enterprises was **65.5 %**, up 0.5 percentage points year-on-year. [Securities Times, 2026-06-30](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3989263.html).
- From January to May, profit of industrial enterprises above designated size increased **18.8 %** year-on-year, with the electronics industry surging **103.9 %** and non-ferrous metals up **117.1 %**, according to the National Bureau of Statistics. [economy.caijing.com.cn, 2026-06-29](http://economy.caijing.com.cn/20260629/5167738.shtml).

**China’s corporate credit conditions remain stable in May** · covered by 2 outlets
- In May 2026, China’s enterprise credit index was **161.53**, indicating generally stable operation, down slightly by 0.88 points from April, according to the State Administration for Market Regulation. [People’s Daily, 2026-06-30](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0630/c1004-40751062.html).
- The top five industries in credit index were finance, electricity/heat/gas/water production and supply, resident services/repair/other services, water conservancy/environment/public facility management, and manufacturing, with resident services entering the top five for the first time this year, according to SAMR. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-06-29](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202606293786569767.html).
