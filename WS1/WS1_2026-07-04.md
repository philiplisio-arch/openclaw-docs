**Regulators seized Wuhan Zhongbang Bank for one year and guaranteed all individual deposits, as the PBOC readied a 1 trillion yuan liquidity operation.**

## At a Glance
- Regulators seized control of **Wuhan Zhongbang Bank** for one year, fully guaranteeing individual deposits. [finance.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-03](http://finance.caijing.com.cn/20260703/5168936.shtml).
- The PBOC will inject **1 trillion yuan** via a 3‑month outright reverse repo on July 6, a net 200 billion yuan after maturities. [Securities Times, 2026-07-03](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3998161.html).
- China will cancel vehicle‑tax breaks for energy‑saving cars, battery‑electric commercial vehicles and plug‑in hybrids from **January 1, 2027**. [szs.mof.gov.cn, 2026-07-03](http://szs.mof.gov.cn/zhengcefabu/202607/t20260703_3992823.htm).

## Top Stories

### Policy
- **Regulators take over Wuhan Zhongbang Bank amid financial risks** · covered by 3 outlets
 - The National Financial Regulatory Administration and the Hubei Provincial People’s Government took over **Wuhan Zhongbang Bank** from July 3, 2026, for a period of one year due to severe credit risks, according to the NFRA and the Hubei government. [finance.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-03](http://finance.caijing.com.cn/20260703/5168936.shtml).
 - Individual deposits are **fully guaranteed** with principal and interest and are unaffected by the takeover, while corporate deposits and interbank liabilities are guaranteed according to the protection plan, according to the NFRA and Hubei government. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-03](http://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-07-03/doc-inifpusu3571814.shtml).
 - During the takeover, **Hankou Bank** will legally undertake the relevant assets, liabilities, business and personnel of Wuhan Zhongbang Bank, according to the NFRA and Hubei government. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-03](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260703/herald/f254f23ba69de69ba9e4fbd5953bbd3b.html).
 - As of end‑2024, Wuhan Zhongbang Bank had total assets of **123.5 billion yuan**, operating revenue of 1.811 billion yuan, net profit of 511 million yuan and a non‑performing loan ratio of 1.5%, according to the bank’s 2024 annual report. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-03](http://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-07-03/doc-inifpusu3571814.shtml).

- **China revises vehicle tax policy for energy‑efficient and new energy cars** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The Ministry of Finance, the State Taxation Administration and the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology jointly announced, with State Council approval, that from **January 1, 2027**, the policy of halving vehicle and vessel tax for energy‑saving cars will be canceled, according to the three ministries. [szs.mof.gov.cn, 2026-07-03](http://szs.mof.gov.cn/zhengcefabu/202607/t20260703_3992823.htm).
 - Also from January 1, 2027, the policy of exempting battery‑electric commercial vehicles, plug‑in hybrid vehicles and fuel‑cell commercial vehicles from vehicle and vessel tax will be canceled, according to the three ministries. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-03](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260703/herald/ab5fb36370a485b0c3cbc0cee351d55a.html).
 - **Battery‑electric passenger cars and fuel‑cell passenger cars** remain exempt from vehicle and vessel tax and are not affected by the adjustment, according to the three ministries. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-03](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260703/herald/ab5fb36370a485b0c3cbc0cee351d55a.html).

- **China issues 15th five‑year plan for circular economy development** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The National Development and Reform Commission (NDRC) issued the 15th Five‑Year Plan for Circular Economy Development, with State Council approval, according to the NDRC. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-03](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260703/6314751.shtml).
 - By 2030, **main resource productivity** will increase by about **16%** compared to 2025, annual comprehensive utilization of bulk solid waste will reach about **4.5 billion tons**, and annual recycling of major renewable resources will reach **510 million tons**, according to the plan. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-03](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260703/6314751.shtml).
 - By 2030, the output value of the resource recycling industry will reach **8 trillion yuan**, and by 2035 a high‑quality circular economy development system will be basically established, according to the plan. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-04](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607043793963141.html).

- **Platform economy urged to shift from price competition to quality and innovation** · covered by 1 outlet
 - The State Administration for Market Regulation issued draft rules prohibiting long‑term large subsidies on takeout platforms to exclude competition, according to the authority. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-03](http://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-07-03/doc-inifnnxn0559371.shtml).
 - The Central Economic Work Conference at end‑2025 proposed deeply rectifying **‘involution‑style’ competition**, and the ‘Network Trading Platform Rules Supervision and Management Measures’ and ‘Internet Platform Anti‑monopoly Compliance Guidelines’ have been implemented, according to the authority. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-03](http://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-07-03/doc-inifnnxn0559371.shtml).

### Macro
- **PBOC injects 1 trillion yuan via outright reverse repo** · covered by 4 outlets
 - The People’s Bank of China will conduct **1 trillion yuan** of buyout reverse repo operations on July 6, with a term of 3 months (91 days), to maintain ample liquidity in the banking system, according to the PBOC. [Securities Times, 2026-07-03](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3998161.html).
 - With 800 billion yuan of the same tenor buyout reverse repo maturing this month, the operation achieves a **net injection of 200 billion yuan**, ending three consecutive months of contraction, according to Wang Qing, chief macro analyst at Dongfang Jincheng (东方金诚; a Chinese credit rating agency). [finance.ce.cn, 2026-07-03](http://finance.ce.cn/bank12/scroll/202607/t20260703_3067739.shtml).
 - The central bank launched the buyout reverse repo tool in October 2024, according to the PBOC. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-03](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260703/herald/42033e540a54aad427e058f2196417c9.html).

- **ECB’s Nagel stresses caution on next rate decision** · covered by 1 outlet
 - ECB Governing Council member Joachim Nagel stated that the ECB should remain vigilant on inflation risks and maintain policy flexibility, calling the current situation **very volatile** and noting that oil‑price declines surprised the market, according to Nagel. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-04](http://finance.sina.com.cn/stock/usstock/c/2026-07-04/doc-inifqfhq3510189.shtml).
 - The ECB raised borrowing costs in June, becoming the first G7 central bank to hike rates due to the Iran war, and Nagel said the June action was unavoidable because data showed inflation was still too high even in mild scenarios, according to Nagel. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-04](http://finance.sina.com.cn/stock/usstock/c/2026-07-04/doc-inifqfhq3510189.shtml).
