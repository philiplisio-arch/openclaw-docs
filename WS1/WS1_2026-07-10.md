## Top Stories

### Company

- **ChangXin Memory Technologies sets IPO subscription for July 16, 377 employees join strategic placement** · covered by 2 outlets
 - ChangXin Memory Technologies (长鑫科技; a Chinese DRAM manufacturer) disclosed its prospectus for a STAR Market IPO on July 9, with new stock subscription starting on **July 16**, according to the Shanghai Stock Exchange. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-09](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260709/herald/a5cf8e40c28822513badef15b5a17723.html).
 - The company plans to raise **29.5 billion yuan**, making it the second largest IPO on the STAR Market in history, with an initial offering of 6.688 billion shares (about 10% of post-issue equity). [stock.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-09](http://stock.caijing.com.cn/20260709/5169964.shtml).
 - **377 core employees** will participate in the strategic placement, which accounts for 3.344 billion shares, or 50% of the initial offering. [stock.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-09](http://stock.caijing.com.cn/20260709/5169964.shtml).

- **Enflame Technology gets CSRC approval for STAR Market IPO** · covered by 2 outlets

[m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-09](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260709/herald/dc7b8bab8bac20ec5e85a0bd321608e7.html).
 - The company's IPO status on the Shanghai Stock Exchange has changed to "registration effective." [Securities Times, 2026-07-09](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4009795.html).

- **Industrial Fulian forecasts H1 profit up 93%–101% on AI server demand** · covered by 1 outlet
 - Industrial Fulian (工业富联; a Chinese electronics manufacturer) expects H1 2026 net profit to increase **93%–101%** year-on-year, reaching 23.4–24.4 billion yuan. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-09](http://m.21jingji.com/timeline/0105657fef34866ec822e18fe16e292a.html).
 - Growth was driven by cloud-provider AI server revenue up over **230%** year-on-year and shipments of data center switches above 800G up 1.4 times year-on-year. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-09](http://m.21jingji.com/timeline/0105657fef34866ec822e18fe16e292a.html).

### Trade

- **China's new energy vehicle exports surge 120% in first half of 2026** · covered by 1 outlet
 - China's new energy vehicle exports in H1 2026 reached **2.355 million units**, a year-on-year increase of **120%**, according to the China Association of Automobile Manufacturers. [People's Daily, 2026-07-10](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0710/c1004-40757133.html).
 - Total auto exports in H1 2026 were **5.096 million units**, up **65.3%** year-on-year, while domestic auto sales fell **4.1%** to 15.017 million units and NEVs accounted for 49.6% of new car sales. [People's Daily, 2026-07-10](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0710/c1004-40757133.html).

### Policy

- **PBOC MPC reaffirms moderately loose monetary policy stance** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The People's Bank of China Monetary Policy Committee held its second quarter 2026 regular meeting on **July 4**, deciding to continue implementing **moderately loose monetary policy** and increase counter-cyclical and cross-cyclical adjustments, according to the PBOC. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-09](http://finance.china.com.cn/money/bank/20260709/6315493.shtml).
 - The committee emphasized maintaining ample liquidity and keeping social financing and money supply growth aligned with economic growth and price-level targets, and called for enhancing the resilience of the foreign exchange market to keep the renminbi exchange rate basically stable at a reasonable and balanced level. [economy.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-09](http://economy.caijing.com.cn/20260709/5169849.shtml).

- **PBoC relending program lifts financing for small and micro enterprises** · covered by 1 outlet
 - The People's Bank of China established a special relending program for private enterprises in early 2026 with a quota of **1 trillion yuan**; the outstanding balance is now about **700 billion yuan**, according to the central bank. [People's Daily, 2026-07-10](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0710/c1004-40757168.html).
 - Guangdong Province had cumulatively disbursed **103.3 billion yuan** in private enterprise relending loans by the end of May. [People's Daily, 2026-07-10](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0710/c1004-40757168.html).

- **China launches national summer cultural and tourism consumption season** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The 2026 National Summer Cultural and Tourism Consumption Season was launched in Qinghai on **July 8**, with over **30,000 cultural and tourism consumption events** scheduled nationwide and more than **450 million yuan** in consumption vouchers issued, according to the Ministry of Culture and Tourism. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-09](http://finance.china.com.cn/industry/20260709/6315558.shtml).
 - Qinghai Province alone issued **20 million yuan** in cultural tourism consumption vouchers covering tickets, accommodation, travel routes, and transportation. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-08](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-08/4460772.html).

- **Chinese ministries launch initiatives to foster retail innovation** · covered by 1 outlet
 - China's retail sales of goods grew from 39.4 trillion yuan in 2021 to **44.3 trillion yuan** in 2025, a cumulative growth of **12.5%**, accounting for about 90% of total consumer goods retail sales, according to the Ministry of Commerce. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-09](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260709/6315577.shtml).
 - Pilot projects for new consumption formats are being carried out in **50 cities**, with over 6,000 projects collected expected to drive more than **300 billion yuan** in social investment, and the first batch of 8.6 billion yuan in support funds is being disbursed. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-09](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260709/6315577.shtml).

- **China targets stable urban employment during 15th Five-Year Plan period** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The 15th Five-Year Plan for Human Resources and Social Security Development sets a target of re-employing **25 million** urban unemployed workers and keeping the urban surveyed unemployment rate within **5.5%**, according to the Ministry of Human Resources and Social Security. [economy.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-09](http://economy.caijing.com.cn/20260709/5169984.shtml).
 - The plan also aims to keep the basic pension insurance participation rate above **95%** and bring the scale of enterprise annuity funds to exceed **9 trillion yuan**. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-09](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607093799298536.html).
