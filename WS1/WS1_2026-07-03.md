### Trade

 - The first meeting, held June 29 in Brussels between Chinese Commerce Minister Wang Wentao and EU Trade Commissioner Šefčovič, established four working areas: trade and investment balance, export controls, intellectual property, and WTO reform. [Securities Times, 2026-07-01](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3994622.html).
