## Top Stories
### Markets
- **China 'national team' increases holdings of domestic stocks; many A-share firms forecast profit growth** · covered by 3 outlets
 - China Guoxin Holdings (a state-owned investment firm) has used over **50 billion yuan** of special refinancing and supporting funds for stock buybacks and increases to stabilize the market, according to the company. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-19](http://finance.sina.com.cn/stock/marketresearch/2026-07-19/doc-iniikaqx3371822.shtml).
 - China Chengtong Group (中国诚通; another state-owned enterprise) has accumulated nearly **10 billion yuan** in purchases of Chinese stock assets and will continue using its own funds and stock buyback refinancing loans to increase holdings of state-owned enterprise and technology company stocks and ETFs, according to the company. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-19](http://finance.sina.com.cn/stock/marketresearch/2026-07-19/doc-iniikaqx3371822.shtml).

- **Over 900 Shenzhen-listed firms issue H1 earnings forecasts, tech sector leads** · covered by 2 outlets
 - As of July 18, 2026, **900** companies on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange have disclosed their first-half 2026 performance forecasts or express reports, accounting for 31% of all listed companies and 33% of total market capitalization. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-20](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-20/4477301.html).
 - The nonferrous metals, chemicals, non-bank financial, and AI computing-related industries showed particularly strong performance growth, with upstream resource cyclical industries experiencing simultaneous volume and price increases and AI computing demand continuing to surge, according to Wang Kai of an unnamed institution. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-20](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-20/4477301.html).

- **Moutai raises prices; China to resume consumption tax on some batteries** · covered by 4 outlets
 - Three ministries — the Ministry of Finance, the General Administration of Customs, and the State Administration of Taxation — issued a notice to adjust the consumption tax policy on certain batteries. [Securities Times, 2026-07-19](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4026913.html).
 - From September 1, 2026, a **2%** consumption tax will apply to certain batteries including mercury-free primary batteries and nickel-metal hydride batteries, increasing to **4%** from September 1, 2027; from April 1, 2027, a **2%** tax will be levied on photovoltaic cells, rising to **4%** from April 1, 2028, according to the three ministries. [Securities Times, 2026-07-19](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4026913.html).

- **China private equity fund positions hit record high in early July** · covered by 1 outlet
 - As of July 10, the stock private equity position index rose for three consecutive weeks, hitting a new high in nearly four years, according to the data platform Simuwang. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-20](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-20/4477303.html).

### Company
- **Transsion Holdings expects H1 net profit up 45% YoY** · covered by 6 outlets
 - Transsion Holdings (传音控股; a Chinese smartphone maker) expects net profit attributable to parent for the first half of 2026 to be **1.756 billion yuan**, an increase of **44.82%** year-on-year, according to the company. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-19](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-19/4477243.html).
 - The performance change is mainly due to continuous growth in storage prices, leading the company to adjust product pricing based on cost changes, resulting in a higher average selling price of smartphones and improving gross margin, the company said. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-19](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-19/4477243.html).
 - Biological Co., Ltd. (生物股份; a Chinese animal vaccine maker) expects its net profit attributable to parent for the first half of 2026 to increase by **50.60% to 80.39%** year-on-year, according to its mid-year performance forecast released July 6, 2026. [Economic Observer, 2026-07-18](http://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0718/963543.shtml).
 - Medicilon (美迪西; a Chinese drug research service firm) expects H1 2026 net profit attributable to parent to be **42-63 million yuan**, turning a loss into profit year-on-year, with revenue growing approximately **40%** YoY, attributed to a recovery in global innovative drug R&D demand. [Jiemian, 2026-07-19](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14791253.html).

- **Changxin Technology IPO draws 7.7 million winning subscriptions** · covered by 3 outlets
 - Changxin Memory Technologies (长鑫科技; a Chinese DRAM manufacturer) announced **7,702,207** winning numbers for its A-share IPO, each entitling the holder to purchase 500 shares at an issue price of **8.66 yuan** per share, with the final online issuance winning rate at **0.47141739%**, according to the company. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-19](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-19/doc-iniiirzf0217077.shtml).
 - CXMT is the largest DRAM manufacturer in China and fourth globally, with a global market share of approximately **7.7%** in 2025, according to Omdia. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-19](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-19/doc-iniiirzf0217077.shtml).
 - The company expects H1 2026 revenue between **110 billion and 120 billion yuan**, up 612.53%-677.31% YoY, and net profit attributable to shareholders between **50 billion and 57 billion yuan**, up 2244.03%-2544.19%, according to the company. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-19](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-19/doc-iniiirzf0217077.shtml).
 - Co-lead underwriters CICC and CITIC Securities each invested **1 billion yuan** as follow-on investment, and four insurance companies (China Life, PICC, China Post Life, Taikang Life) each received about **11.5473 million shares** worth about **100 million yuan** in the strategic placement, totaling about **400 million yuan**, according to the Securities Times. [Securities Times, 2026-07-19](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4027583.html).

- **Qunketec unveils AI video creation agent at World AI Conference** · covered by 4 outlets
 - Qunketec (群核科技; a Chinese AI company) unveiled a new AI video creation agent at the 2026 World Artificial Intelligence Conference. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-18](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260718/herald/41340a29f484a341dd38b0fb0bfad193.html).

[m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-18](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260718/herald/41340a29f484a341dd38b0fb0bfad193.html).
 - Yixin (易鑫; a Chinese auto-finance platform) showcased its AI-powered car finance processing machine, reducing processing time from 90 minutes to **15-20 minutes**, with an intelligent risk control system achieving an average approval time of **8 seconds**, according to the company. - **Hua'an Securities chairman proposes share buyback after 26% monthly drop** · covered by 3 outlets
 - Hua'an Securities (华安证券; a Chinese brokerage) chairman Zhang Hongtao (章宏韬) proposed a share buyback of **1 to 2 billion yuan** to maintain company value and protect shareholder rights, according to the company. [Jiemian, 2026-07-19](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14790547.html).
 - The buyback price shall not exceed **150%** of the average trading price of the company's A shares in the 30 trading days prior to the board resolution, and repurchased shares will be sold via centralized bidding 12 months after disclosure or canceled if unsold within three years. [Jiemian, 2026-07-19](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14790547.html).

- **ZTO Express receives ESG award; JPMorgan increases stake by 333,000 shares** · covered by 1 outlet
