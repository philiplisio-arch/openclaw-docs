## Top Stories

### Company

- **CSRC approves Unitree Technology's STAR Market IPO registration** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The China Securities Regulatory Commission (CSRC) approved Unitree Technology (宇树科技; a Chinese humanoid-robot developer)'s IPO registration on the STAR Market on July 1, 2026, with the approval valid for **12 months**; the company must report major events to the Shanghai Stock Exchange during the effective period, according to the CSRC. [finance.ce.cn](http://finance.ce.cn/stock/gsgdbd/202607/t20260702_3065432.shtml).
 - Unitree's IPO registration was completed **104 days** after acceptance, setting a record for the fastest review under the pre-screening mechanism, and it plans to raise **4.202 billion yuan** at an estimated valuation of **42 billion yuan**. [finance.eastmoney.com](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607023792050496.html).
 - Unitree shipped over **5,500 humanoid robots** in 2025, the highest globally; revenues grew from 159 million yuan in 2023 to **1.699 billion yuan** in 2025, with non-GAAP net profit reaching 591 million yuan in 2025. [finance.eastmoney.com](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607023792050496.html).

- **Huayi Microelectronics terminates IPO, merges into Huatian Technology** · covered by 2 outlets
 - Huatian Technology (华天科技; a domestic integrated-circuit packaging and testing leader) plans to acquire **100% equity** of Huayi Microelectronics (华羿微电; a Chinese semiconductor design and packaging company) for **2.996 billion yuan**, an appraisal premium of **166.17%**, according to the company's restructuring report. [finance.sina.com.cn](http://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-07-02/doc-inifkcsh5995291.shtml).
 - Huayi Micro's design business unit commits net profits of no less than **139 million yuan**, **166 million yuan**, and **189 million yuan** for 2026, 2027, and 2028 respectively; its packaging and testing unit commits cumulative net profit to be positive over the period. [Securities Times](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3993828.html).

- **Nayuki Holdings turns into penny stock as shareholders question losses** · covered by 3 outlets
 - Nayuki Holdings (奈雪的茶; a Chinese tea-chain operator) fell to **0.65 HKD** per share as of the July 2 trading session, becoming a penny stock, and has dropped over **96%** from its IPO price of 19.8 HKD per share. [stock.caijing.com.cn](http://stock.caijing.com.cn/20260702/5168472.shtml).
 - Nayuki posted losses in four of five fiscal years after listing and did not achieve profitability in fiscal 2025; average order value at directly operated stores dropped from 43.3 yuan in the first three quarters of 2020 to **24.4 yuan** in 2025. [finance.sina.com.cn](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-02/doc-inifkiyh2654923.shtml).
 - Despite share repurchases of about **4.79 million HKD** in the first half of June, the stock remains under pressure; about 10 individual shareholders attended the annual general meeting, and one questioned why the chairman does not take an annual salary of 1 yuan. [Securities Times](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3994279.html).

- **Pharma company abandons Hong Kong IPO, chair cites long queue** · covered by 1 outlet
 - Nuosiland (诺思兰德; a Chinese biopharmaceutical company) terminated its H-share listing plan and instead plans to raise up to **300 million yuan** through a private placement of A-shares, according to company announcements. [36Kr](http://36kr.com/p/3877776492671233).
 - Chairman Xu Songshan said many companies are queuing for H-share listings, causing long wait times, and that the Beijing Stock Exchange encourages sci-tech enterprises to meet funding needs via onshore refinancing; the company disclosed its Hong Kong listing plan in October 2025 and terminated it on June 30, 2026. [36Kr](http://36kr.com/p/3877776492671233).

- **Estun subsidiary to acquire Nanjing Estun equity** · covered by 2 outlets
 - Estun (埃斯顿; a Chinese industrial-automation company) announced on July 2, 2026 that its wholly-owned subsidiaries Estun Robot and Dingtong Electromechanical plan to acquire equity of Nanjing Estun Co., Ltd. via cash payment, according to company announcements. [Securities Times](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3995716.html).
 - After the transaction, Estun will indirectly hold **100% equity** of Nanjing Estun Co., Ltd., which engages in R&D, production, and sales of collaborative robots (3-35 kg load capacity) and embodied intelligent robots, with downstream applications in automotive, home appliances, 3C electronics, and food logistics. [finance.eastmoney.com](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607023792176196.html).

- **Yonghe Chemical's fluoropolymer business boosts H1 net profit by 69.5% YoY** · covered by 1 outlet
 - Yonghe Co. (永和股份; a Chinese fluorochemical company) expects net profit for the first half of 2026 of **460 million to 550 million yuan**, an increase of **69.51% to 102.68%** year-on-year, attributing the growth to high refrigerant prices and rising volume and price of fluoropolymer materials, according to the company's performance forecast. [Securities Times](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3995443.html).

### Policy

- **New A-share trading rules to take effect next Monday** · covered by 2 outlets
 - A-share trading rules will be implemented on July 6 (next Monday), expanding after-hours fixed-price trading to all A-shares and Shanghai/Shenzhen ETFs, according to the Shanghai, Shenzhen, and Beijing stock exchanges. [m.21jingji.com](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260702/herald/2432f77f8ef52a88e07ce5a62cd42567.html).
 - The price limit for main-board risk-warning stocks (ST and *ST) will be adjusted from **5% to 10%**, the Shanghai Stock Exchange will change the closing trading method for funds from continuous auction to closing call auction, and the Shenzhen Stock Exchange will introduce a market-maker system on the ChiNext board. [finance.eastmoney.com](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607023792066748.html).

### Energy

- **Global EV market enters accelerated growth phase** · covered by 1 outlet
 - The International Energy Agency (IEA) forecasts global EV sales of **23 million units** in 2026, nearly **30%** of new-car sales, and that by 2035 the global EV stock will reach **510 million**, more than six times the 2025 level, with EVs at about 50% of global car sales. [finance.sina.com.cn](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-01/doc-inifheyw4254237.shtml).
 - In Q1 2026, EV sales grew nearly **30%** in Europe, **80%** in Asia-Pacific excluding China, and **75%** in Latin America; from January to May 2026, China exported **1.833 million** new energy vehicles, a 1.1-fold increase year-on-year. [finance.sina.com.cn](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-01/doc-inifheyw4254237.shtml).
 - In April 2026, Brazil's imports of Chinese EVs more than doubled year-on-year, making it the largest single export destination for Chinese EVs, while other Asian countries imported **20%** more, becoming the largest import market. [finance.sina.com.cn](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-01/doc-inifheyw4254237.shtml).

### Markets

- **Tech stocks plunge for second day; semiconductor sector leads decline** · covered by 3 outlets
 - As of the July 2 close, the Shanghai Composite fell **2.03%**, the Shenzhen Component fell **3.85%**, the ChiNext Index fell **5.71%**, and the STAR 50 Index fell sharply by **7.7%**; total turnover in Shanghai and Shenzhen was about **3.45 trillion yuan**, down about 209.5 billion yuan from the previous trading day. [National Business Daily](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-02/4445604.html).
 - The semiconductor sector adjusted sharply, with GigaDevice and other stocks hitting daily downside limits, while the precious-metals sector rose against the market as Zhaojin Gold and Chifeng Gold hit upside limits; 2,219 stocks rose and 3,162 fell, with 156 hitting the daily limit up and 41 the limit down. [Securities Times](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3994620.html) [Jiemian](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14688865.html).

- **New A-share accounts in H1 surge 60% to 20.16 million** · covered by 1 outlet
 - In June 2026, new A-share accounts opened numbered **2.8646 million**, up 3.6% month-on-month and 73.99% year-on-year; in the first half of 2026, cumulative new A-share accounts reached **20.1613 million**, up **60%** year-on-year. [finance.eastmoney.com](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607023792066748.html).
