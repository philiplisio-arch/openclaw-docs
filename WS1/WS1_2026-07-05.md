## Top Stories

### Policy

- **China securities regulator proposes reforms to listed company refinancing rules** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The China Securities Regulatory Commission raised the small-and-fast refinancing cap from 3 billion to **6 billion yuan** for Shanghai and Shenzhen listed companies, to **10 billion yuan** for mega-caps with net assets over 100 billion yuan, and from 1 billion to **2 billion yuan** for Beijing Stock Exchange companies, according to the CSRC. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-04](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-04/4452304.html).
 - The regulator established a shelf registration system allowing listed companies to register once and issue securities in multiple batches over two years, according to the CSRC. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-04](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-04/4452304.html).
 - A unified market-price-based pricing mechanism was mandated for all private placements, using the first day of the offering period as the pricing base date, according to the CSRC. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-03](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-03/doc-inifpuss2419139.shtml).
 - The regulator simplified conditions for controlling shareholders subscribing to share issuances but extended the lock-up period to **36 months**, according to the CSRC. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-03](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-03/doc-inifpuss2419139.shtml).
 - During the 14th Five-Year Plan period, listed companies' refinancing totaled **3.8 trillion yuan**, or 64% of total equity financing, according to the CSRC. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-04](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-04/4452304.html).

- **China's 15th Five-Year Plan targets faster recycling of 'new three' solid wastes** · covered by 3 outlets
 - The National Development and Reform Commission (国家发展改革委) issued the Circular Economy Development '15th Five-Year Plan', targeting an increase in the main resource output rate of about **16%** by 2030 compared with 2025, according to the NDRC. [People's Daily, 2026-07-04](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0704/c1004-40753267.html).
 - By 2030, annual comprehensive utilization of bulk solid waste is targeted at about **4.5 billion tons**, and annual recycling of major renewable resources at **510 million tons**, according to the NDRC. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-04](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260704/6314807.shtml).
 - The plan sets a goal for the output value of the resource recycling industry to reach **8 trillion yuan** by 2030, according to the NDRC. [Securities Times, 2026-07-04](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3998198.html).

- **China issues guidelines for data property rights registration to boost secure and efficient data flow** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The National Data Administration issued the Data Property Rights Registration Work Guidelines (Trial) to promote secure, compliant, and efficient data circulation, according to the National Data Administration. [Jiemian, 2026-07-04](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14701681.html).
 - The guidelines make the national data management department responsible for establishing a unified national data property rights registration system and for building and managing the national data property rights registration service system, according to the National Data Administration. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-04](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607043794183718.html).

- **China clarifies rights and obligations under revised e-commerce law draft** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The State Administration for Market Regulation (国家市场监督管理总局) and the Ministry of Commerce (商务部) released the draft amendment to the E-commerce Law for public comment on July 4, containing **20 articles** covering five main areas, according to the two ministries. [Jiemian, 2026-07-04](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14700955.html).
 - The draft expands the legal scope to clarify rights and obligations of other participants in the platform economy, improves the platform responsibility system, and includes countermeasures to protect enterprise rights, according to the two ministries. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-04](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-04/doc-inifpuss2419139.shtml).

- **China securities regulator outlines measures for risk prevention, stronger supervision, and high-quality development of capital markets** · covered by 1 outlet
 - On July 3, the CSRC Party Committee held a commendation conference where Chairman Wu Qing (吴清) delivered a report on establishing and practicing a correct view of political achievements, according to the CSRC. [finance.ce.cn, 2026-07-04](http://finance.ce.cn/stock/gsgdbd/202607/t20260704_3068618.shtml).
 - The CSRC Party Committee requires all party organizations to work diligently in risk prevention, strengthening regulation, and promoting high-quality development of the capital market, according to the CSRC. [finance.ce.cn, 2026-07-04](http://finance.ce.cn/stock/gsgdbd/202607/t20260704_3068618.shtml).

### Company

- **Tianqi Materials terminates 243,000-ton lithium battery material project** · covered by 1 outlet
 - Tianqi Materials (天赐材料; a Chinese battery-materials maker) terminated the **243,000-ton** lithium battery and fluorine-containing new materials project at its subsidiary Nantong Tianci, which had a planned total investment of **26.54 billion yuan**, according to Tianqi Materials. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-04](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260704/herald/e8f62ae54c42cb2f0458402fd49869de.html).
 - As of June 30, 2026, the construction-in-progress balance for the terminated project was **9.3613 million yuan**, according to Tianqi Materials. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-04](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260704/herald/e8f62ae54c42cb2f0458402fd49869de.html).
 - Tianqi Materials reported 2025 revenue of **16.65 billion yuan** and net profit of **1.362 billion yuan**, up 33% and 181.43% respectively, according to Tianqi Materials. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-04](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260704/herald/e8f62ae54c42cb2f0458402fd49869de.html).

- **Huawei's He Tingbo publishes second version of 'Tao's Law' paper with engineering details and test data** · covered by 2 outlets
 - Huawei (华为; a Chinese tech company) semiconductor head He Tingbo (何庭波) released version 2.0 of the paper 'Time Scaling Theory for Multi-level Electronic Systems' on July 3, 2026, adding mass-production measurement data tables for Kirin 2026 and the baseline Kirin9030 Pro, according to Huawei. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-04](http://finance.sina.com.cn/stock/t/2026-07-04/doc-inifrhua3297719.shtml).
 - The V2 paper elaborates on the gear-ratio concept of core technology LogicFolding, enabling continuous cell-level optimization in 3D design, according to Huawei. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-04](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607043794171129.html).
