## Top Stories

### Policy

- **State Council approves 15th Five-Year Plan for expanding consumption** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The State Council (国务院) approved the 'Expanding Consumption 15th Five-Year Plan', submitted by the National Development and Reform Commission and the Ministry of Commerce as document No. 〔2026〕847. [Jiemian, 2026-07-13](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14753776.html).
 - By 2030, total retail sales of consumer goods are targeted to reach approximately **60 trillion yuan**. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-13](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260713/herald/98e8acad8f43cbda58adae157b1f851c.html).
 - The plan includes actions to improve service consumption, enhance catering quality, expand quality domestic service supply, and accelerate inclusive childcare service construction. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-13](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260713/herald/98e8acad8f43cbda58adae157b1f851c.html).
 - It aims to increase the share of service consumption in per capita expenditure and to expand cultural and tourism consumption through 'culture+tourism+industries' integration. [Jiemian, 2026-07-13](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14753776.html).

- **Premier Li Qiang chairs economic symposium with experts and entrepreneurs including Pangdonglai** · covered by 1 outlet
 - Premier Li Qiang chaired an economic symposium at which eight participants spoke, including Huang Hanquan, Yang Zhiyong, Wang Wen, Zhang Jun, Hou Qijun, Yu Donglai, Wang Zhijian, and Li Jun, according to CCTV News. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-13](http://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-07-13/doc-inihspyr7475838.shtml).
 - Huang Hanquan is President of the Chinese Academy of Macroeconomic Research (NDRC); Yang Zhiyong is President of the Chinese Academy of Fiscal Sciences; and Wang Wen is Dean of the Chongyang Institute for Financial Studies at Renmin University of China. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-13](http://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-07-13/doc-inihspyr7475838.shtml).
 - Hou Qijun is Chairman of Sinopec Group; Yu Donglai is Chairman of Henan Pangdonglai (a Chinese supermarket chain); Wang Zhijian is Chairman of Shandong Heavy Industry Group; and Li Jun is Chairman of Sugon (a Chinese high-performance computing company). [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-13](http://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-07-13/doc-inihspyr7475838.shtml).

- **14 departments launch three-year action to expand rehabilitation aids industry** · covered by 1 outlet
 - 14 departments including the Ministry of Civil Affairs jointly issued the 'Rehabilitation Assistive Device Industry Capacity Expansion and Quality Improvement Three-Year Action Plan (2026-2028)', aiming by 2028 for the number of rehabilitation assistive device enterprises above designated size nationwide to exceed **500**, according to the Ministry of Civil Affairs. [People's Daily, 2026-07-14](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0714/c1004-40759843.html).

- **China launches pilot for integrity-based grading supervision in markets** · covered by 1 outlet
 - The State Administration for Market Regulation (市场监管总局) approved **9** provincial market regulation departments to pilot a credit-based tiered classification supervision scheme for fair trade in markets, with the pilot period ending on June 30, 2027. [People's Daily, 2026-07-14](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0714/c1004-40759844.html).

### Macro

- **High-frequency data signals resilient H1 economic growth** · covered by 3 outlets
 - Offline commercial foot traffic grew **5.7%** year-on-year in the first half of 2026, and offline consumption payment amount rose **2.7%**, with electronic goods consumption up **9.5%**, according to the State Information Center of the National Development and Reform Commission. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-13](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260713/herald/e2fb46658b8573651305f4ec86555c8b.html), [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-13](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607133802716491.html).
 - Investment in frontier fields like AI and humanoid robots surged **118.4%** year-on-year in the first half, and bid amounts for digital infrastructure projects like computing power increased **23.0%** year-on-year. [People's Daily, 2026-07-13](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0713/c1004-40759046.html).
 - The third batch of 'two major' construction projects allocated **193.5 billion yuan** in ultra-long-term special treasury bonds. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-13](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260713/herald/e2fb46658b8573651305f4ec86555c8b.html).

### Company

- **Ximai Food expects H1 net profit up to 1.55B yuan, up 90% YoY** · covered by 3 outlets
 - Ximai Food (西麦食品; a Chinese oat-food maker) expects H1 2026 net profit attributable to parent of **139 million to 155 million yuan**, up 70.58% to 90.21% year-on-year, driven by product and channel innovation and lower procurement costs from a bumper grain harvest. [industry.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-13](http://industry.caijing.com.cn/20260713/5170675.shtml).
 - Zhibang Home (志邦家居; a Chinese home-furnishings company) forecasts a net loss attributable to shareholders of **126 million to 189 million yuan** for H1 2026. [estate.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-13](http://estate.caijing.com.cn/20260713/5170701.shtml).
 - Xin Hua Insurance (新华保险; a Chinese life insurer) expects H1 2026 net profit attributable to shareholders of **20.719 billion to 23.678 billion yuan**, up 40% to 60% year-on-year. [finance.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-13](http://finance.caijing.com.cn/20260713/5170704.shtml).

- **Zhongji Innolight denies rumors of severe Q2 earnings miss and stock manipulation** · covered by 3 outlets
 - Zhongji Innolight (中际旭创; a Chinese optical-transceiver maker) denied rumors that its Q2 earnings severely missed expectations and that it intentionally suppressed its stock price, stating it has not yet released a performance forecast or semi-annual report. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-13](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-13/doc-inihrhfi4209158.shtml), [Securities Times, 2026-07-13](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4015766.html).
 - The company expressed confidence in its 2026 first-half business and industry demand, with the interim report expected to be disclosed on August 24, 2026. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-13](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607133802739517.html).

- **Zijin Mining advances lithium and molybdenum projects; plans interim dividend** · covered by 1 outlet
 - Zijin Mining (紫金矿业; a Chinese metals miner) said the first phase of its Manono lithium mine smelting project is scheduled to be completed and put into operation in **December 2026**, and announced an interim dividend of **4.2 yuan per 10 shares**. [Economic Observer, 2026-07-14](http://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0714/956555.shtml).
 - Its Anhui Shapinggou molybdenum mine, the world's largest single molybdenum deposit, began construction of its beneficiation project in mid-June, with an expected annual output of about **22,100 tons** of molybdenum. [Economic Observer, 2026-07-14](http://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0714/956555.shtml).

- **Tianji shares to face other risk warnings from July 15** · covered by 2 outlets
 - Tianji Co., Ltd. (天际股份; a Chinese lithium battery materials maker) received a 'Notice of Ex-ante Administrative Penalty' from the Guangdong Securities Regulatory Bureau (广东证监局) for false records in its 2023 annual report, and its stock will carry an 'ST' risk warning from July 15, with the daily price limit unchanged at **10%**. [Securities Times, 2026-07-13](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4015766.html).
 - The bureau intends to fine Tianji **2 million yuan** and four executives a total of **3.55 million yuan**. Separately, Tianji expects H1 2026 net profit of **220 million to 260 million yuan**, reversing a year-earlier loss, attributed to shifts in supply and demand for its main product lithium hexafluorophosphate from Q4 2025. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-07-14/doc-inihsyqu5498536.shtml).

- **Guangzhou Bank closes credit card center; consolidates under head office** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The National Financial Regulatory Administration Guangdong Regulatory Bureau approved the termination of Guangzhou Bank's (广州银行) credit card center, with the bank integrating the credit card business into the head office's retail finance segment for unified management. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-13](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-13/4470251.html).
 - Guangzhou Bank must surrender the financial license to the bureau within **15 working days**, and has established a 'Credit Card Department' to handle all credit card businesses, debts, and contracts. [finance.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-13](http://finance.caijing.com.cn/20260713/5170538.shtml).

### Trade

- **Chinese cooling appliance exports surge in Europe** · covered by 1 outlet
 - China's air conditioner exports to the EU reached **3.76 billion USD** in the first half, up 43.2% year-on-year, according to the General Administration of Customs. [People's Daily, 2026-07-14](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0714/c1004-40759863.html).
 - Midea's total air conditioner sales in Western Europe grew over **70%** year-on-year in the first half, with its portable split air conditioner selling over 60,000 units in Germany. [People's Daily, 2026-07-14](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0714/c1004-40759863.html).
 - Chinese air conditioner brand market share in Europe rose from **27%** in 2023 to **41%**. [People's Daily, 2026-07-14](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0714/c1004-40759863.html).

### Markets

- **China's futures market shows dual increase in H1 volume and turnover** · covered by 2 outlets
 - In the first half of 2026, China's national futures market saw a dual increase in cumulative volume and turnover, according to the China Futures Association. [CCTV, 2026-07-12](http://jingji.cctv.com/2026/07/12/ARTIXcN0BTSeOVhdBizTYv4b260712.shtml).
 - In June alone, national futures market volume (single-sided) was **9.39 billion lots** with turnover of **80.62 trillion yuan**, up 27% and 52.72% year-on-year respectively. [finance.ce.cn, 2026-07-13](http://finance.ce.cn/futures/qhgdbd/202607/t20260713_3083221.shtml).
