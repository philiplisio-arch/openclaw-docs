## Top Stories

### Company
- **ICBC Halts Precious Metals Bidding as Banks Tighten Gold Business** · covered by 2 outlets
    - Industrial and Commercial Bank of China (ICBC, China’s largest commercial bank) will stop its agency personal precious metals trading business from **July 24, 2026**, according to ICBC [National Business Daily, 2026-06-25](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-06-25/4436877.html).
    - Postal Savings Bank of China stopped its agency personal precious metals business on **February 11, 2026**, requiring customer self-liquidation by March 13, according to Postal Savings Bank [National Business Daily, 2026-06-25](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-06-25/4436877.html).
    - China Construction Bank raised the standard margin for deferred contracts to **120%** in early June 2026, according to China Construction Bank [National Business Daily, 2026-06-25](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-06-25/4436877.html).
    - International spot gold price fell below **$4,000 per ounce** as of June 24, down over $1,400 from its year high, according to the article [National Business Daily, 2026-06-25](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-06-25/4436877.html).
    - Goldman Sachs cut its gold year-end target to **$4,900 per ounce**, a reduction of $500, according to Goldman Sachs [National Business Daily, 2026-06-25](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-06-25/4436877.html).

### Markets
- **Chinese Banks Raise Precious Metals Margin Requirements to 140%** · covered by 3 outlets
    - ICBC raised the margin ratio for Au(T+D), mAu(T+D), and Ag(T+D) deferred contracts to **140%** on June 18, according to ICBC [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-25](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260625/herald/acd43b5a9fbcffd2b6ce859ebc60c4de.html).
    - Huaxia Bank raised the margin ratio for gold deferred contracts from **35%** to **120%** and for Ag(T+D) from 42% to 120%, according to Huaxia Bank (a Chinese commercial bank) [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-25](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260625/herald/acd43b5a9fbcffd2b6ce859ebc60c4de.html).
    - Analyst Wu Zewei states that a margin ratio exceeding 100% means the leverage level of personal precious metal deferred trading has essentially returned to zero, according to analyst Wu Zewei [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-25](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260625/herald/acd43b5a9fbcffd2b6ce859ebc60c4de.html).
    - Guangfa Bank raised margin requirements for precious metals deferred trading to **140%**, according to Guangfa Bank [Caixin, 2026-06-23](http://finance.caixin.com/2026-06-23/102456614.html).

### Property
- **Tech Wealth Drives Luxury Housing Purchases in China** · covered by 2 outlets
    - By the end of 2025, the post-90s generation accounted for **31%** of luxury home buyers in Shenzhen (units over 10 million yuan), according to 乐有家研究中心 [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-25](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260625/herald/f65c6d4febb7f94bec143f915a9a755d.html).
    - In Q1 2026, Shenzhen sold **168** luxury homes worth over 30 million yuan, up **154.55%** year-on-year, according to 克而瑞 [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-25](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260625/herald/f65c6d4febb7f94bec143f915a9a755d.html).
    - The Hangzhou project '奥映世纪轩' sold **86** units at an average price of 87,681 yuan per square meter, with one unit attracting 27 groups competing, achieving a single-day sales volume of **2.06 billion yuan**, according to market data [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-25](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260625/herald/f65c6d4febb7f94bec143f915a9a755d.html).
    - The 'tech new rich' are concentrated in industries such as chips, AI, smart manufacturing, new energy vehicles, and semiconductors, according to a project official [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-25](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260625/herald/f65c6d4febb7f94bec143f915a9a755d.html).
