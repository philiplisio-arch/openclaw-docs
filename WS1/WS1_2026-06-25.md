### Macro
- **PBOC to Conduct 500 Billion Yuan MLF Operation** · covered by 4 outlets
    - The People's Bank of China will conduct a **500 billion yuan** MLF operation on June 25, 2026, according to the People's Bank of China. [Jiemian](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14640679.html).
    - The MLF operation has a **one-year term**, according to the People's Bank of China. [Jiemian](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14640679.html).
    - The operation uses a fixed quantity, interest rate bidding, and multiple-price award method, according to the People's Bank of China. [Jiemian](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14640679.html).
    - The purpose is to maintain ample liquidity in the banking system, according to the People's Bank of China. [Jiemian](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14640679.html).

### Trade
- **MOFCOM Implements Supply Chain Security Investigation Measures** · covered by 1 outlet
    - The Ministry of Commerce issued and implemented the 'Working Measures for Investigation of Industrial and Supply Chain Security', according to the Ministry of Commerce. [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746807.html).
    - The measures were released on **June 24, 2026**. [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746807.html).
    - The measures consist of **22 articles**, according to the Ministry of Commerce. [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746807.html).
    - The measures specify the objects, content, procedures, and handling measures of the investigation, according to the Ministry of Commerce. [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746807.html).
    - The measures were formulated to implement the State Council's regulations on industrial and supply chain security, according to the State Council. [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746807.html).
    - The measures aim to safeguard the security and stability of China's and global industrial and supply chains, according to the Ministry of Commerce. [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746807.html).

    - The online reporting platform is accessible at **https://aqygzj.mofcom.gov.cn**, according to the Ministry of Commerce. [Jiemian](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14639721.html).
    - The reporting hotline is **010-12369**, available on workdays from 8:30 to 11:30 and 14:00 to 17:00, according to the Ministry of Commerce. [Jiemian](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14639721.html).
    - The announcement will take effect from **July 1, 2026**, according to the Ministry of Commerce. [Jiemian](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14639721.html).
    - The announcement was issued on **June 24, 2026**, according to the Ministry of Commerce. [Jiemian](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14639721.html).
