### Policy

- **Financial regulator issues new rules to govern AI safety in financial institutions** · covered by 1 outlet
    - The National Financial Regulatory Administration (金融监管总局) issued the ‘Guiding Opinions on the Safe Development and Application of Artificial Intelligence in Banking and Insurance’, requiring financial institutions to establish a full lifecycle management system for AI applications. [People's Daily, 2026-06-25](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0625/c1004-40747361.html).
    - High-risk applications, including those involving fund transactions, credit approval, underwriting claims, and generative AI scenarios directly related to customer interests, must strengthen access management. [People's Daily, 2026-06-25](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0625/c1004-40747361.html).
    - Personal data such as name, ID number, phone number, and bank card number cannot be used for training generative AI models. [People's Daily, 2026-06-25](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0625/c1004-40747361.html).

- **Regulator guides insurance industry to handle summer grain claims** · covered by 2 outlets
    - From January to May 2026, national agricultural insurance claims paid **370 billion yuan**, benefiting **12.09 million households**, according to the National Financial Regulatory Administration (国家金融监督管理总局). [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-25](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260625/herald/57c1f3e1eb626aebace5b27cc6dfd433.html).
    - Grain crop insurance paid **6.047 billion yuan**, benefiting **5.4631 million households**, of which wheat insurance paid **3.093 billion yuan**, benefiting **3.4342 million households**. [People's Daily, 2026-06-25](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0625/c1004-40747412.html).
    - The regulator held a video scheduling meeting with 8 major summer grain producing provinces and 8 major property insurance companies. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-25](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260625/herald/57c1f3e1eb626aebace5b27cc6dfd433.html).
