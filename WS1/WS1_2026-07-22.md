## Top Stories

### Macro

- **China's industrial output rises 5.4% in H1, contributes over 35% to economic growth** · covered by 4 outlets
 - In the first half of 2026, the added value of industrial enterprises above designated size increased **5.4%** year-on-year, with equipment manufacturing and high-tech manufacturing growing **9.3%** and **13.3%** respectively, according to the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (MIIT). [Securities Times, 2026-07-21](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4031092.html).
 - Profits of industrial enterprises above designated size rose **18.8%** year-on-year in the first five months, while goods trade exports in RMB terms grew **13.4%** in the first half, according to MIIT Chief Engineer Wang Weiming. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-21](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260721/6316980.shtml).
 - The application adoption rate of AI technology in manufacturing enterprises above designated size exceeded **30%**, and China's intelligent computing power reached **2,185 EFLOPS (FP16)** as of end-June, with overall rack utilization of computing facilities at **71.4%**, said MIIT spokesperson Xie Cun. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-20](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-20/4481075.html).
 - China achieved a historic breakthrough in reusable rocket technology with successful controlled recovery of the Long March 10B carrier rocket's first stage, and became the world's first country to reach **100-ton** annual production of T1200-grade ultra-high strength carbon fiber, according to MIIT. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-21](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260721/6316980.shtml).

### Policy

- **PBOC adds new relending quotas for agriculture and small firms** · covered by 3 outlets
 - The People's Bank of China (中国人民银行; China's central bank) added **100 billion yuan** in relending quotas to support flood relief and post-disaster reconstruction, targeting disaster-affected regions including Guangxi, Hubei, Gansu, and Zhejiang, according to the PBOC.
