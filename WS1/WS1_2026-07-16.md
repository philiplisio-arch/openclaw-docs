## Top Stories

### Policy
- **PBOC's Zou Lan: Improve short-term rate mechanism, gradually increase overnight reverse repo frequency** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The temporary repo and reverse repo tool band was narrowed to a symmetric **50 bps** around the policy rate, according to Zou Lan, Deputy Governor and Spokesperson of the People's Bank of China (PBOC). [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-15](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260715/herald/7093543651876cb1f3637fd5118df23f.html).
 - The central bank will study gradually increasing the frequency of overnight reverse repo operations based on primary dealer needs, and will continue to smoothly promote reform of the monetary policy tool operation framework, Zou Lan said. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-15](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-15/4473308.html).
 - The operation window for temporary repo and reverse repo was advanced to 3:00-3:30 PM, and the overnight rate DR001 at end-June was 1.36%, according to Zou Lan. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-15](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260715/herald/7093543651876cb1f3637fd5118df23f.html).

- **2026 China Private Enterprise Social Responsibility Report released** · covered by 1 outlet
 - The All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce released the report on July 14, based on a survey of **102,800** private enterprises; listed private firms had R&D intensity 1.6 pp higher than the market average. [People's Daily, 2026-07-16](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0716/c1004-40761449.html).
 - Chinese private enterprises held 6 of the top 10 global new energy vehicle sales seats, and the top 5 wind turbine installed-capacity manufacturers are all private firms, according to the report. [People's Daily, 2026-07-16](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0716/c1004-40761449.html).

### Trade
- **China's H1 imports exceed 10 trillion yuan for the first time, up 22.1%** · covered by 2 outlets
 - In the first half of 2026, China's imports reached **10.74 trillion** yuan, a historical first exceeding 10 trillion in a half-year, up 22.1% year-on-year, according to Customs Deputy Director Wang Jun. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-14](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260714/herald/acb5d6c6ea9027d34f2051ea50f3e9c9.html).
 - Import growth exceeded export growth by **8.7 percentage points**; imports of metal ores grew 22.6% and electronic components grew 45.6%, while edible oils (+19.2%) and aquatic products (+24.1%) also rose. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260714/6316117.shtml).
 - China has implemented zero-tariff policies for **63** countries, and imports from over 150 countries and regions achieved growth; China has been the world's second-largest import market for 17 consecutive years, with its share of global imports rising from 7.9% to about 10%, Wang Jun noted. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260714/6316117.shtml).

### Company
- **Tianzhihang plans to acquire MicroPort's overseas orthopedics business** · covered by 2 outlets
 - On July 15, Tianzhihang (天智航; a Chinese surgical-robot company) announced it will acquire a controlling stake in Shanghai MicroPort Orthopedics via share issuance, expected to constitute a major asset restructuring without changing the actual controller. [Securities Times, 2026-07-16](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4021794.html).
 - In 2025, Tianzhihang achieved revenue of **2.79 billion** yuan (+55.9%) and added 49,000 surgeries; cumulative surgeries exceeded 160,000 by end-March 2026. [Securities Times, 2026-07-16](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4021794.html).
 - Shanghai MicroPort Orthopedics operates in over 70 countries with overseas business in the U.S., Japan and Europe; its internal-axis knee product has a clinical history of over 20 years with more than 1 million implants globally. [Securities Times, 2026-07-16](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4021794.html).

 - The pricing was confirmed alongside other July 15 evening announcements. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-15](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607153807443461.html).

[m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-14](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260714/herald/1a6350bdcae1efca45df6daf32e75858.html).

### Macro
- **China's H1 retail sales grow 1.3%, online consumption leads** · covered by 2 outlets
 - In the first half of 2026, total retail sales of consumer goods reached **24.8722 trillion** yuan, up 1.3% year-on-year, according to the National Bureau of Statistics (NBS). [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-15](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260715/herald/447887b43f3de4d266ac4398c9975ed6.html).
 - Excluding automobiles, H1 retail sales rose 2.8%; national online retail sales of goods and services reached 10.0715 trillion yuan, up **5.2%** year-on-year, according to the NBS. [Securities Times, 2026-07-15](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4018832.html).
 - The State Council approved the '15th Five-Year Plan for Expanding Consumption', proposing total retail sales reach about **60 trillion** yuan by 2030. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-15](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260715/herald/447887b43f3de4d266ac4398c9975ed6.html).

- **H1 economy in reasonable range as new momentum grows** · covered by 2 outlets
 - China's H1 2026 GDP grew **4.7%** to 69.5704 trillion yuan, while fixed-asset investment (ex-rural households) fell 5.7% and infrastructure investment declined 2.4%, according to the NBS. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-15](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260715/herald/8e9c787d7aa665dcd7c451307569d420.html).
 - High-tech manufacturing value-added grew **14%** year-on-year in Q2, new growth drivers contributed over 40% to H1 growth, and CPI rose 1.0% while PPI turned positive at 1.5% in H1, according to the NBS. [People's Daily, 2026-07-15](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0715/c1004-40761176.html).
 - Auto retail sales fell **16.1%** year-on-year in June, and the surveyed urban unemployment rate averaged 5.2% in H1. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-15](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260715/herald/8e9c787d7aa665dcd7c451307569d420.html).
