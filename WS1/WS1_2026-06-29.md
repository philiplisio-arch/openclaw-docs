**Ferrari's all-electric Luce enters China at 3.988 million yuan, undercutting Europe, while Abu Dhabi sovereign fund MGX targets GDS' overseas data center platform DayOne for billions, and China's electronic materials sector profits surge 665.4%.**

## At a Glance
- Ferrari launched its first all-electric model **Luce** in China at a starting price of **3.988 million yuan**, about 8% lower than the European price of approximately 4.35 million yuan. [Economic Observer, 2026-06-29](http://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0629/933888.shtml).

[Economic Observer, 2026-06-29](http://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0629/933842.shtml).
- China's electronic special material manufacturing profits surged **665.4%** year-on-year from January to May, with the electronics industry contributing 43.1% of total industrial profit growth. [National Business Daily, 2026-06-28](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-06-28/4439799.html).

### Tech
- **Electronic materials firms' profits surge 665.4% YoY in Jan-May; AI servers consume 3-10x more materials** · covered by 2 outlets
    - From January to May 2026, profits of **electronic special material manufacturing enterprises** above designated size surged **665.4%** year-on-year, according to the National Bureau of Statistics. [National Business Daily, 2026-06-28](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-06-28/4439799.html).
    - The computer, communication and other electronic equipment manufacturing sector contributed **43.1%** to total industrial profit growth, with total profits of industrial enterprises above designated size up **18.8%** YoY. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-06-28](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202606283785618875.html).
    - Economist **Wan Zhe** attributed the profit surge to pricing power, a low base last year, the AI computing power explosion, and accelerated domestic substitution, and noted that AI servers consume approximately **3 to 10 times** the electronic materials of ordinary servers. [National Business Daily, 2026-06-28](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-06-28/4439799.html).
    - Wan Zhe warned of potential overcapacity after 2027 due to capacity expansion by overseas giants, and said the supply-demand gap for HBM memory is expected to persist at least until 2027. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-06-28](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202606283785618875.html).

### Markets
- **Private banks tighten supply of medium/long-term deposits, shift from price competition to service** · covered by 1 outlet
    - **Beijing Zhongguancun Bank** (a Chinese private bank) suspended 3-year time deposit products from May 30, and multiple banks have removed 5-year fixed deposit products from their mobile apps. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-28](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260628/herald/a9ac7840766fe3419f3c13e6b0b15d5d.html).
    - Private banks' net interest margin fell to **3.62%** at the end of Q1 2025, down 0.21 percentage points from end of 2024, according to the National Financial Regulatory Administration. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-28](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260628/herald/a9ac7840766fe3419f3c13e6b0b15d5d.html).
    - **Lou Feipeng** of Postal Savings Bank stated that removing medium- and long-term deposit products is a proactive liability structure optimization by banks, and **Xue Hongyan** of Sushang Bank said it results from regulatory guidance to adjust deposit rates and curb high-interest deposit risks. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-28](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260628/herald/a9ac7840766fe3419f3c13e6b0b15d5d.html).
