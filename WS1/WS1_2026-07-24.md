### Macro
- **PBOC injects 500 billion yuan via MLF to maintain liquidity** · covered by 6 outlets
 - The People's Bank of China (中国人民银行) conducted a **500 billion yuan** Medium-term Lending Facility (MLF) operation on July 24, 2026, with a net injection of **100 billion yuan** as 400 billion yuan matured. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-24](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607233819349185.html).
 - The PBOC also conducted **2.4 trillion yuan** of reverse repos via outright purchase this month, net injecting **700 billion yuan** after offsetting maturing repos, according to the PBOC. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-24](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607233819349185.html).
 - Total medium-term liquidity injection in July reached **800 billion yuan**, reversing the previous four-month contraction, according to the central bank. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-23](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260723/herald/9d4c3995edb3ccb0f409ea2e91727834.html).
 - Ming Ming (明明), Chief Economist of CITIC Securities (中信证券), attributed the reduced net MLF injection to stable bank long-term liability pressure, while expert Dai Bingjie said the operation helps maintain ample liquidity and supports government bond issuance. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-24](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607233819349185.html).

### Company
- **Changxin Memory Tech sets July 27 for STAR Market listing** · covered by 4 outlets
 - Changxin Memory Technologies (长鑫科技; a Chinese DRAM maker) will list on the STAR Market of the Shanghai Stock Exchange on **July 27, 2026**, with an IPO price of **8.66 yuan per share** and total share capital of **66.881 billion shares** before over-allotment, according to the company. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-23](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-23/4504787.html).
 - The company expects H1 2026 net profit attributable to shareholders between **50 billion and 57 billion yuan**, and is valued at approximately **580 billion yuan** upon listing. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-23](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607233819076938.html).
 - Changxin is the largest storage company in China and the fourth largest globally. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-23](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607233819076938.html).

[stock.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-23](http://stock.caijing.com.cn/20260723/5173026.shtml).
 - The expected fundraising amount is about **1.2 billion yuan**, with 550 million yuan for multi-legged robot R&D and 250 million yuan for humanoid robot technology, according to the company's plan. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-23](http://finance.sina.com.cn/stock/hkstock/marketalerts/2026-07-23/doc-iniitzpk7738523.shtml).
 - Yuejiang's Hong Kong-listed shares (02432) opened over **5%** higher in morning trade on July 23, with shares at HK$26.50. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-23](http://finance.sina.com.cn/stock/hkstock/marketalerts/2026-07-23/doc-iniitzpk7738523.shtml).

### Trade
- **High-tech FDI accounted for record share of China's inbound investment in H1** · covered by 3 outlets
 - In the first half of 2026, China's actual use of foreign capital reached **4021.4 billion yuan**, with the decline narrowing by 10.2 percentage points year-on-year, while high-tech industry foreign investment grew by **33.2%** and accounted for a record **42.4%** of total foreign investment, according to Meng Huating, Director of the Foreign Investment Department of the Ministry of Commerce. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-23](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260723/6317488.shtml).
 - New foreign-funded enterprises increased by **5.3%** year-on-year, and modern services sector accounted for **57%** of foreign investment, with electronic and communication equipment manufacturing growing by 52%, technology achievement conversion services by 57.1%, and R&D and design services by 82%, according to the same official. [People's Daily, 2026-07-23](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0723/c1004-40766694.html).
 - The Ministry will accelerate the revision of regulations on foreign investors' mergers and acquisitions of domestic enterprises. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-23](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260723/6317488.shtml).

- **Chinese shipbuilders hold 62.2% of global completions and 82.3% of new orders in H1** · covered by 1 outlet
 - In the first half of 2026, China's shipbuilding completion volume reached **36.5 million deadweight tons**, a year-on-year increase of 51.2%, accounting for **62.2%** of the world total, while new ship orders were **121.06 million deadweight tons**, up 173.1%, representing **82.3%** of the global total, according to the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology. [People's Daily, 2026-07-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0724/c1004-40766868.html).
 - China's share of global green ship new orders has exceeded **68%** for three consecutive years and remains high in the first half of 2026, according to the China Association of the National Shipbuilding Industry. [People's Daily, 2026-07-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0724/c1004-40766868.html).
 - Guangzhou Shipyard International has a contract value of nearly **100 billion yuan**, with over 95% of orders international and production scheduled through 2030. [People's Daily, 2026-07-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0724/c1004-40766868.html).

- **Chinese tech services going global: big data and AI exports surge** · covered by 1 outlet
 - Over the past 5 years, China's service trade exports have grown at an average annual rate of **14%**, and in the first 5 months of 2026, service exports increased by **21%** year-on-year, with computer information services, business services, and intellectual property services revenue growing by 17% and contributing 45% of service export growth, according to the State Administration of Foreign Exchange. [People's Daily, 2026-07-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0724/c1004-40766864.html).
 - Neusoft's global in-vehicle intelligent travel solution version 7.0 received **15** cooperation intentions from the international market within a month of release. [People's Daily, 2026-07-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0724/c1004-40766864.html).

### Policy
- **China's trade-in program drives 1.1 trillion yuan in sales** · covered by 5 outlets
 - The consumer goods trade-in policy drove **1.1 trillion yuan** in sales in the first half of 2026, benefiting **150 million people**, according to the Ministry of Commerce (商务部). [economy.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-22](http://economy.caijing.com.cn/20260722/5172774.shtml).
 - Under the policy, **3.707 million vehicles** were traded in, **63.266 million home appliances**, and **79.098 million digital and smart products** were purchased new. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-22](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607223817230226.html).
 - In June 2026, new energy vehicles accounted for **65.4%** of vehicles receiving trade-in subsidies, and the retail penetration rate of new energy vehicles reached a record **62.4%** in Q2 2026. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-22](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607223817230226.html).

- **Shanghai issues measures to boost direct financing for tech firms** · covered by 3 outlets
 - Shanghai issued "Several Measures to Strengthen Sci-Tech Financial Services" on July 23, comprising **20 measures** in five categories, including establishing a Shanghai Social Security Sci-Tech Innovation Fund and forming specialized sub-funds managed by professional market investment institutions, according to the Shanghai Financial Committee Office (上海市委金融委员会办公室). [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-23](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260723/herald/30e230e363a9b368d3fc5f44007bd34b.html).
 - The measures include exploring an S-fund-of-funds to attract more S-funds and upgrading the Shanghai Angel Investment Association to version 2.0, as well as expanding the application of the STAR Market's fifth set of listing standards to AI and low-altitude economy. [Jiemian, 2026-07-23](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14814409.html).
 - As of end of June 2026, Shanghai STAR Market companies raised a total of **237.636 billion yuan** in IPOs with a total market value of **3.99 trillion yuan**. [Jiemian, 2026-07-23](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14814409.html).

- **China customs rolls out measures to cut trade costs, boost efficiency** · covered by 1 outlet
 - In the first half of 2026, China's foreign trade imports and exports increased by **16.9%** year-on-year, with exports up 13.4% and imports up 22.1%, and exports of high-tech products grew by **39%**, electric vehicles by **68.7%**, and integrated circuits by **88.7%**, according to the General Administration of Customs (海关总署). [Xinhua, 2026-07-23](http://www.news.cn/tech/20260723/b0132f7882e040ec9407f3ed4b76758b/c.html).
 - Customs has implemented measures such as compressing sea-rail container interchange time to within **half an hour** and reducing transshipment costs by **20%**, and shortening export lithium battery quality inspection from 48 hours to **30 minutes**. [Xinhua, 2026-07-23](http://www.news.cn/tech/20260723/b0132f7882e040ec9407f3ed4b76758b/c.html).

- **CSRC convenes party-building symposium, no new regulatory measures disclosed** · covered by 7 outlets
 - The China Securities Regulatory Commission (CSRC) held a meeting on party building and regulatory work on July 23, 2026, with Chairman **Wu Qing** attending, outlining seven key tasks including maintaining market stability, deepening reforms, strict regulation, and promoting medium- and long-term funds to increase market entry. [www.csrc.gov.cn, 2026-07-23](http://www.csrc.gov.cn/csrc/c100028/c7647101/content.shtml).
 - Separately, Wu Qing met with Canada Pension Plan Investment Board (CPPIB) CEO John Graham on July 21, stating that the CSRC will steadily expand high-level institutional opening and improve convenience for foreign capital participation; CPPIB said it will continue to actively invest in China. [stock.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-23](http://stock.caijing.com.cn/20260723/5172904.shtml).

### Energy
- **China sets 2030 energy consumption target at 1.8 billion tons of standard coal** · covered by 5 outlets
 - The 15th Five-Year Plan for Renewable Energy Development, jointly issued by the National Development and Reform Commission (国家发展改革委) and the National Energy Administration (国家能源局), targets total renewable energy consumption of about **1.8 billion tons of standard coal** by 2030, and total installed renewable power capacity of about **3.5 billion kW** with annual generation of **6 trillion kWh**. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-23](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260723/6317430.shtml).
 - Wind and solar installed capacity is targeted at least **2.8 billion kW** (over 50% of total), generating over **4 trillion kWh** (30% of total), and cumulative offshore wind new construction starts will reach about **100 GW** by 2030. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-24](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607243819362667.html).
 - Investment in the renewable energy industry during the 15th Five-Year Plan is expected to exceed **5 trillion yuan** in total. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-23](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260723/6317430.shtml).

[finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-23](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-23/doc-iniiusme9479405.shtml).

 - The policies also promote integration of intelligent terminals and agents, including developing new smartphones, smart glasses, and smart robots. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-23](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607233818963524.html).
