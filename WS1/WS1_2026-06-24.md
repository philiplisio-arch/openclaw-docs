### Trade
- **Consumer Trade-In Program Drives 5 Trillion Yuan in Sales, Autos Account for 63%** · covered by 2 outlets
    - The consumer goods trade-in program has driven total sales of **5 trillion yuan** and benefited **630 million** person-times, according to the Ministry of Commerce. [CCTV, 2026-06-24](http://jingji.cctv.com/2026/06/24/ARTIqfUFv34j5BmSdJWc66q5260623.shtml).
    - Automobile trade-in sales account for **63%** of the total, with over **21 million** people supported to exchange for new cars and over **12 million** new energy vehicles supported. (source: 商务部) [CCTV, 2026-06-24](http://jingji.cctv.com/2026/06/24/ARTIqfUFv34j5BmSdJWc66q5260623.shtml).
    - The average subsidy per car was **14,000 yuan**, and over **40%** of passenger vehicle retail sales benefited from trade-in subsidies, according to Ministry of Commerce Market Operation and Consumption Promotion Department Director Yang Mu. [National Business Daily, 2026-06-23](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-06-23/4434967.html).
    - Carbon dioxide emissions were reduced by at least **23 million tons**. (source: 商务部市场运行和消费促进司司长杨沐) [National Business Daily, 2026-06-23](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-06-23/4434967.html).

- **China-Europe Freight Train Scale Continues to Rise, 10.8 Times Growth** · covered by 1 outlet
    - As of June 2026, the unified brand of China-Europe Railway Express has been in use for **10 years**, and the annual number of trains increased by **10.8 times** over that period, with the transport network basically covering the whole of Eurasia, according to China State Railway Group. [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746218.html).

(source: 国家统计局) [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746388.html).
