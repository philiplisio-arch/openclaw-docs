### Policy

- **Multiple Ministries Detail Policies to Stabilize and Optimize Foreign Capital Utilization** · covered by 6 outlets
    - Foreign investment in wholesale and retail increased by **22.6%** year‑on‑year from January to May, according to Vice Minister of Commerce Ling Ji. [China Daily, 2026-06-23](http://caijing.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202606/23/WS6a3a18fda310d709c2fb98e7.html).

- **40 Cities Selected for Consumption Reform Pilot, Clear Roadmap to Boost Auto Sales** · covered by 5 outlets

[Securities Times, 2026-06-23](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3974181.html).
