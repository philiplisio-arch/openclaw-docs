### Property
- **Real estate market maintains stable trend** · covered by 1 outlet
    - From January to May, the total transaction volume of new and second-hand houses nationwide decreased by **2.1%** year-on-year, with the decline narrowing by **2.7 percentage points** from the first quarter, according to the National Bureau of Statistics and the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development. [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746388.html).
    - In May, the number of cities with year-on-year growth in total new and second-hand housing transactions was **191**, of which **77** cities including Wuhan, Zhengzhou, and Ningbo saw growth exceeding 20%. [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746388.html).
    - In May, the new commercial housing sales price in first-tier cities rose **0.2%** month-on-month, with the increase expanding by **0.1 percentage point**. [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746388.html).
    - In May, the average sales price of new commercial housing nationwide was **10,500 yuan per square meter**, up **5.0%** year-on-year. [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746388.html).
    - In May, Beijing's second-hand housing online signing volume was **15,974** units, the highest for the same period in nearly five years. [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746388.html).
    - In May, new listings of second-hand housing in 25 key cities were **120,000** units, down about **41%** year-on-year, according to China Index Academy. [People's Daily, 2026-06-24](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0624/c1004-40746388.html).
