## Top Stories

### Policy

- **China forex regulator to roll out policy package to ease cross-border investment** · covered by 3 outlets
 - The State Administration of Foreign Exchange will simplify capital account settlement and payment procedures for foreign-invested enterprises, according to SAFE Capital Market Department Director Xiao Sheng. [People's Daily, 2026-07-17](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0717/c1004-40762949.html).
 - SAFE will also expand the applicable subject scope of cross-border financing facilitation policies and promote the green foreign debt pilot nationwide. [People's Daily, 2026-07-17](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0717/c1004-40762949.html).
 - In the first half of 2026, banks' cross-border receipts and payments on behalf of customers totaled **9.2 trillion USD**, a record high for the period, up **21%** year-on-year, according to SAFE Deputy Director Li Bin. [Securities Times, 2026-07-17](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4024682.html).
 - SAFE plans to issue a new round of **QDII quotas** soon to support residents' overseas securities investment, Xiao Sheng said. [People's Daily, 2026-07-17](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0717/c1004-40762949.html).

- **China's NDRC releases action plan for AI cooperation development** · covered by 4 outlets
 - The National Development and Reform Commission released the Artificial Intelligence Cooperative Development Action Plan, promoting cross-border data flow and cross-border trusted data spaces. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-17](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260717/6316593.shtml).
 - The plan includes eight actions: high-quality data supply, intelligent computing power inclusiveness, open-source ecosystem sharing, deep AI empowerment, digital talent co-cultivation, rule and standard co-building, security governance cooperation, and AI for good. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-18](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607183811448425.html).
 - The Smart Computing Power Inclusiveness Action aims to provide affordable intelligent computing services to developing countries. [Securities Times, 2026-07-17](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4025010.html).
 - The plan also encourages co-building an international AI open-source community and sharing of general models, basic algorithms, and tool components. [Securities Times, 2026-07-17](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4025010.html).

- **China to adjust battery consumption tax in phases** · covered by 5 outlets
 - The Ministry of Finance (财政部), General Administration of Customs, and State Taxation Administration jointly issued an announcement: from September 1, 2026, certain batteries (including mercury-free primary cells, Ni-MH, lithium primary, lithium-ion, and vanadium redox flow) will be taxed at **2%**, rising to **4%** from September 1, 2027. [People's Daily, 2026-07-18](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0718/c1004-40763081.html).
 - Photovoltaic batteries will be taxed at **2%** from April 1, 2027, and at **4%** from April 1, 2028. [People's Daily, 2026-07-18](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0718/c1004-40763081.html).
 - From September 1, 2026 to December 31, 2028, sodium-ion batteries, solid-state batteries, fuel cells, and certain photovoltaic cells (perovskite, tandem, gallium arsenide) are exempt from consumption tax. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-17](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-17/doc-iniiccuw7372186.shtml).

- **China's macro policy front-loaded and effective, mid-year review says** · covered by 1 outlet
 - On January 15, the People's Bank of China announced **8 policy measures**, lowering interest rates on various structural monetary policy tools and expanding support. [People's Daily, 2026-07-18](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0718/c1004-40763061.html).
 - As of June 20, consumer goods trade-in sales exceeded **1 trillion yuan** this year, benefiting **136 million person-times**, with a subsidy leverage ratio of **1:10.3**. [People's Daily, 2026-07-18](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0718/c1004-40763061.html).
 - This year, **250 billion yuan** in ultra-long-term special government bonds were allocated for consumer goods trade-ins, and a **100 billion yuan** fiscal-financial coordination fund was set up to boost domestic demand. [People's Daily, 2026-07-18](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0718/c1004-40763061.html).

- **China market watchdog says 99.05% of frozen drinks pass quality check** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The State Administration for Market Regulation organized a nationwide food safety sampling inspection of frozen drinks, with a pass rate of **99.05%** across **6,415 batches** from **581 production enterprises** in all 31 provinces. [People's Daily, 2026-07-17](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0717/c1004-40762966.html).
 - The inspection covered all categories including ice cream, popsicles, and edible ice, testing **32 indicators** including food additives and microbiological indexes. [industry.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-17](http://industry.caijing.com.cn/20260717/5171837.shtml).

### Macro

- **Henan province H1 GDP grows 5.0% year-on-year** · covered by 10 outlets
 - Henan province's GDP reached **3.352158 trillion yuan** in the first half of 2026, growing **5.0%** year-on-year, according to the Henan Provincial Bureau of Statistics and NBS Henan Survey Office. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-17](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-17/4475933.html).
 - The secondary industry added value rose **5.4%**, while the tertiary industry grew **4.9%** and the primary industry **4.0%**. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-17](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-17/4475933.html).
 - Nationally, China's GDP grew **4.7%** year-on-year in H1 2025 (reference data released earlier), with high-tech manufacturing value added up **13.3%**. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-16](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260716/6316396.shtml).

- **Foreign investment in China overall improving, official data shows** · covered by 1 outlet
 - In the first half of 2026, high-tech services and high-tech manufacturing foreign capital inflow grew **61%** year-on-year, according to the State Administration of Foreign Exchange. [People's Daily, 2026-07-18](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0718/c1004-40763062.html).
 - Cross-border capital by non-bank sectors recorded a net inflow of **247.2 billion USD** in the first half. [People's Daily, 2026-07-18](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0718/c1004-40763062.html).
 - The domestic RMB foreign exchange market transaction volume totaled **22.1 trillion USD** in H1, up **5%** year-on-year. [People's Daily, 2026-07-18](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0718/c1004-40763062.html).

 - Another **26 projects** with total capacity of **3.2 GW** are under construction; unit cost has fallen from about **30,000 yuan/kW** to **15,000 yuan/kW** over the past decade. [People's Daily, 2026-07-17](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0717/c1004-40762310.html).
