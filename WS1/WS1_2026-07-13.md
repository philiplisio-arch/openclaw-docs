## Top Stories

### Company
- **State Council Accelerates Digital China; Helium Exports Banned; Changxin Memory Plans Record 29.5B Yuan IPO** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The State Council (国常会) executive meeting decided to systematically advance the construction of Digital China, accelerating the build-out of next-generation communication networks and computing power networks, according to the State Council. [21jingji, 2026-07-13](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260713/herald/9bf4c6b981505800a9d37d641a53387b.html).
 - The Ministry of Commerce and the General Administration of Customs decided to impose a temporary ban on the export of helium, according to the two bodies. [Securities Times, 2026-07-13](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4013172.html).
 - Sinopec (a Chinese oil and petrochemical giant) completed the restructuring of China Aviation Oil, making it a wholly-owned subsidiary, according to Sinopec. [21jingji, 2026-07-13](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260713/herald/9bf4c6b981505800a9d37d641a53387b.html).
 - Changxin Memory Technologies (长鑫科技; a Chinese memory chipmaker) plans to raise **29.5 billion yuan** in its IPO subscription opening on Thursday, making it the largest A-share IPO this year, according to the company. [Securities Times, 2026-07-13](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4013172.html).
- **Hengtong Co. to invest up to 600M yuan in Indonesia digital park** · covered by 2 outlets
 - Hengtong Co., Ltd. (恒通股份; a Chinese logistics and port operator) plans to invest no more than **600 million yuan** to build a digital smart industrial park in Indonesia, according to the company. [Securities Times, 2026-07-13](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4013123.html).
 - The park, in the Galang Batang Special Economic Zone on Bintan Island, will focus on smart logistics park operations, data storage and equipment leasing, information system integration, and digital technical services, according to the company. [Jiemian, 2026-07-13](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14747085.html).
 - Hengtong expects first-half net profit attributable to the parent of between 166 million and **203 million yuan**, a year-on-year increase of **66.94% to 104.03%**, according to the company. [Securities Times, 2026-07-13](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4013123.html).
- **Western Gold's controlling shareholder to merge with Xinjiang Geology and Mining Investment Group** · covered by 2 outlets
 - Western Gold (西部黄金; a Chinese gold mining company) said its controlling shareholder intends to merge wholly with Xinjiang Geological and Mining Investment Group (新疆地矿投资集团), with no significant impact on production and the actual controller remaining the State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission of Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region; the merger is still subject to higher-level approval and involves uncertainty, according to the company. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-12](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-12/4468463.html).
 - Xinjiang Xinxin Mining (新疆新鑫矿业) received notice from its controlling shareholder about a proposed strategic reorganization with Xinjiang Geological and Mining Investment Group, which does not involve major asset restructuring or a change of controlling shareholder, according to the company. [East Money, 2026-07-12](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607123802647810.html).

### Markets
- **Multiple top Chinese funds increase stakes in Hong Kong-listed innovative drug stocks; National Essential Drug List adds 16 innovative drugs** · covered by 1 outlet
 - Fullgoal Fund increased its stake in Baiao Saitu to **7%**, triggering a margin call, and E Fund raised its stake to over **7%**, according to the Hong Kong Stock Exchange. [21jingji, 2026-07-12](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260712/herald/55439b454711f84fee4e711dbe1c752e.html).
 - China Asset Management increased its stake in H&H International Holdings to **5.02%**, triggering a margin call, and Harvest Fund raised its stake in 3SBio to **7%** and in CSPC Pharmaceutical Group to **5%**, according to the Hong Kong Stock Exchange. [21jingji, 2026-07-12](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260712/herald/55439b454711f84fee4e711dbe1c752e.html).
 - The National Health Commission and two other departments published the 2026 edition of the National Essential Drug List, adding **16 innovative drugs**, according to the National Health Commission. [21jingji, 2026-07-12](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260712/herald/55439b454711f84fee4e711dbe1c752e.html).
 - The Hang Seng Innovative Drug Index rose **nearly 19%** over nine trading days from June 29 to July 11. [21jingji, 2026-07-12](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260712/herald/55439b454711f84fee4e711dbe1c752e.html).
- **Institutions: tech sector adjustment nearly over, look to H1 reports for new themes** · covered by 1 outlet
 - Last week's A-share volatility centered on the AI sector adjustment, with the recent tech-stock pullback largely driven by fund flows and overseas market sentiment, according to brokerages. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-13](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-13/4468723.html).
 - Profit-taking contraction in related sectors is nearly sufficient, suggesting the bottom of this adjustment may be near, while the long-term trend of AI computing demand expansion remains unchanged and AI is still expected to be the leading theme, according to brokerages. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-13](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-13/4468723.html).
 - Semi-annual reports released intensively in mid-to-late July will be a key variable for verifying market rhythm, according to brokerages. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-13](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-13/4468723.html).

### Trade
- **Zhuji embroidery machine exports thrive as niche industry** · covered by 1 outlet
 - The entire industrial chain of Zhuji's embroidery machine industry has an output value exceeding **15 billion yuan**, with the machines accounting for about **90%** of the domestic market and about **80%** of the global market, and the industry has grown at over **20% annually** for six consecutive years. [People's Daily, 2026-07-13](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0713/c1004-40759002.html).
 - By 2008, there were over **300** embroidery machine companies in Zhuji, and two-thirds were eliminated during the price war, according to Chen Zhenming, president of the Zhuji Computer Embroidery Machine Industry Association. [People's Daily, 2026-07-13](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0713/c1004-40759002.html).

### Policy
- **Hebei Province unveils comprehensive education reform plan, including free preschool and AI empowerment** · covered by 1 outlet
 - Hebei Province will deepen the '1+4' comprehensive education reform, cultivate virtue and talents, tap red resources, and cooperate with universities in Beijing and Tianjin on faculty, discipline building, and technology transfer, according to Hebei Province. [People's Daily, 2026-07-13](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0713/c1004-40758991.html).
 - The plan includes accelerating the construction of Xiong'an campuses for relocated universities and expanding basic education through free preschool education and a county middle school revitalization plan, while exploring AI to empower education reform, according to Hebei Province. [People's Daily, 2026-07-13](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0713/c1004-40758991.html).
