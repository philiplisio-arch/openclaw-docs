### Geopolitics
- **China and EU establish formal trade and investment consultation mechanism** · covered by 2 outlets
    - The first meeting of the mechanism was held on June 29, 2026, co-chaired by Chinese Commerce Minister Wang Wentao and EU Trade and Economic Security Commissioner Maroš Šefčovič, according to the Ministry of Commerce. [Economic Observer, 2026-06-30](http://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0630/935394.shtml).
    - The mechanism is built on four pillars: trade and investment balance, export controls, intellectual property, and the World Trade Organization. [Economic Observer, 2026-06-30](http://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0630/935394.shtml).
    - Both sides exchanged market access lists and agreed to set up a joint monitoring mechanism to exchange trade data. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-06-29](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202606303787109659.html).
    - China expressed concerns over the EU inverter financing ban, the Cybersecurity Law amendment draft, and the Industrial Accelerator Act, while Šefčovič stated the EU does not intend to expand trade frictions. [Economic Observer, 2026-06-30](http://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0630/935394.shtml).

### Company
- **Wanda Plaza asset sale accelerates as multiple investors take stakes in malls** · covered by 2 outlets
    - Shanghai Anya Enterprise Management Partnership (a Chinese investment partnership) changed its equity; Suzhou Anyi subscribed **21.98 billion yuan**, gaining 99.9995% control. [Jiemian](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14667501.html).

- **Bank AIC investments in hard-tech startups accelerate, with over 58 projects in H1** · covered by 3 outlets
    - In the first half of 2026, nine bank AICs invested in over **58** projects, approaching the total for 2024, according to the Securities Times. [Securities Times, 2026-06-29](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3986281.html).
    - The first three joint-stock bank AICs have all been approved to commence operations; CITIC Bank's AIC (Xin Yin Jin Tou) was approved with a registered capital of **10 billion yuan** and headquarters in Guangzhou. [Securities Times, 2026-06-29](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3986281.html).
    - All five large state-owned banks' AICs appear in the shareholder lists of ChangXin Memory Technologies (a Chinese memory chip maker) and Yangtze Memory Technologies. [Securities Times, 2026-06-29](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3986281.html).
- **Shengda Resources expects H1 net profit up 399% YoY** · covered by 4 outlets
    - Shengda Resources (a Chinese mining company) expects net profit attributable to shareholders for H1 2026 to be RMB **350-400 million**, up 399.31%–470.64% year-on-year. [Securities Times, 2026-06-29](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3987397.html).
    - The core driver is the "four resonances" of technical renovation, capacity release, volume-price rise, and incremental projects; Inner Mongolia Jinshan Mining's major technical renovation achieved full results. [Securities Times, 2026-06-29](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3987397.html).
    - The company and its actual controller were placed under investigation, causing the stock price to hit the daily limit down, according to Jiemian. [Jiemian](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14667151.html).

    - The State Administration for Market Regulation (SAMR) fined Zhongshan Torch Group (an investment company) and Shanghai Dinghui Baifu Investment Management **1.75 million yuan** each for implementing a concentration without approval during the review period.
