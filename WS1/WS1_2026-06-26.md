### Policy
- **China Releases 15th Five-Year Plan for New Energy System** · covered by 5 outlets
    - The National Development and Reform Commission and the National Energy Administration released the plan on June 25, aiming to initially build a clean, low-carbon, safe, and efficient new energy system by **2030**. [People's Daily, 2026-06-26](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0626/c1004-40748036.html).
    - By 2030, the share of new energy power generation is targeted to reach **30%**, according to the National Development and Reform Commission and the National Energy Administration. [National Business Daily, 2026-06-25](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-06-25/4437771.html).
    - Hou Wenjie, director of the Statistics and Digital Intelligence Department of the China Electricity Council, stated that average annual investment in power sources and grids is expected to reach **3 trillion yuan** over the next five years, doubling from the 14th Five-Year Plan period. [People's Daily, 2026-06-26](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0626/c1004-40748036.html).
    - The plan encourages private enterprises to participate in major energy project construction and investment. [People's Daily, 2026-06-26](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0626/c1004-40748036.html).
- **Private Banks Face Pressures on Both Loan and Deposit Sides** · covered by 2 outlets

[China Daily, 2026-06-26](http://caijing.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202606/26/WS6a3df3afa310d709c2fba44f.html).
    - Chongqing Fumin Bank suspended acceptance of new deposits for personal 2-year and longer-term products via online channels from June 15. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-06-26](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-06-26/doc-iniesqzz7384828.shtml).

- **PBOC to Conduct Overnight Reverse Repo at End of June** · covered by 3 outlets

### Energy
- **15th Five-Year Energy Key Projects Investment to Exceed 20 Trillion Yuan** · covered by 3 outlets
    - Total investment in key energy projects and new business forms during the 15th Five-Year Plan period will exceed **20 trillion yuan**, according to the National Energy Administration. [China Daily, 2026-06-26](http://caijing.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202606/26/WS6a3df49ca310d709c2fba451.html).
    - By 2030, China aims to preliminarily establish a clean, low-carbon, safe, and efficient new energy system, with comprehensive energy production capacity reaching **5.8 billion tons** of standard coal. [finance.eastmoney.com](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202606263784544834.html).
    - By 2030, non-fossil energy consumption will account for **25%**, wind and solar power installed capacity will exceed **50%**, and non-fossil energy power generation will account for **50%**. [finance.eastmoney.com](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202606263784544834.html).
    - Key tasks include optimizing the layout of four bases and distributed sources, and strengthening oil and gas strategic security bases, as stated by National Energy Administrator Wang Hongzhi. [Jiemian](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14650974.html).
