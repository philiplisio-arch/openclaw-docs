### Energy
- **Energy investments in 15th Five-Year Plan to exceed 20 trillion yuan** · covered by 3 outlets
    - Total investment in China’s **15th Five-Year Plan** key energy projects and new business forms will exceed **20 trillion yuan**, according to the National Energy Administration. [China Daily, 2026-06-26](http://caijing.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202606/26/WS6a3df49ca310d709c2fba451.html).

- **China issues 15th Five-Year Plan for new energy system** · covered by 5 outlets

[Securities Times](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3980615.html).
    - Pumped storage hydropower installed capacity will reach **160 GW**, new energy storage installed capacity will reach **300 GW**, and nuclear power capacity in operation will reach about **110 GW**. [Securities Times](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3980615.html).

[m.21jingji.com, 2026-06-26](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260626/herald/4dc27b310b890868491963d53f9a9392.html).
