### Policy
- **China issues 15th Five-Year Plan for new energy system** · covered by 5 outlets
    - The plan, released by the National Development and Reform Commission and the National Energy Administration, targets comprehensive energy production capacity of **5.8 billion tons** of standard coal and a non-fossil energy consumption share of **25%** by 2030, according to the commissions. [People's Daily, 2026-06-26](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0626/c1004-40747695.html).
