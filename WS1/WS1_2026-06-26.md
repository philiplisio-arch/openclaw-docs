### Policy
- **China Issues 15th Five-Year Plan for New Energy System** · covered by 5 outlets
    - The National Development and Reform Commission and the National Energy Administration released the 15th Five-Year Plan for building a new energy system, aiming to preliminarily establish a clean, low-carbon, safe, and efficient new energy system by **2030**, according to the two bodies. [People's Daily, 2026-06-26](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0626/c1004-40747695.html).
    - By 2030, comprehensive energy production capacity will reach **5.8 billion tons of standard coal**, and the proportion of non-fossil energy consumption will reach **25%**. [People's Daily, 2026-06-26](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0626/c1004-40747695.html).
    - By 2030, combined wind and solar installed capacity will exceed **50%** of total, and the proportion of electricity generated from non-fossil sources will reach **50%**. [National Business Daily, 2026-06-25](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-06-25/4437771.html).
    - As of the end of May 2026, China's total installed power generation capacity exceeded **4.01 billion kilowatts**, ranking first globally. [National Business Daily, 2026-06-25](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-06-25/4437771.html).
    - By 2030, new grid investment during the 15th Five-Year Plan period will exceed **5 trillion yuan**, and new West-to-East power transmission capacity will increase by over **80 million kilowatts**. [Securities Times, 2026-06-25](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3980380.html).
    - By 2030, the plan targets **1.6 billion kW** of pumped storage hydropower, **300 million kW** of new energy storage, and **2 million tons** of renewable hydrogen production. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-06-26](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202606263783486691.html).
