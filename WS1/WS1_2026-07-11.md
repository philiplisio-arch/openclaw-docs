## Top Stories

### Trade
- **China imposes temporary export ban on helium, a key semiconductor material** · covered by 2 outlets
 - China's Ministry of Commerce (商务部) and General Administration of Customs (海关总署) imposed a temporary ban on helium exports, according to the two bodies. [www.gelonghui.com](http://www.gelonghui.com/p/5533293).
 - The ban takes effect from the date of announcement, with subsequent adjustments to be announced separately, according to the Ministry of Commerce and General Administration of Customs. [finance.eastmoney.com](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607103801871574.html).
 - Helium is a key raw material in semiconductor manufacturing, accounting for **21%** of global helium demand. [finance.eastmoney.com](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607103801871574.html).

### Policy
- **China releases Energy Efficiency and Carbon Reduction Action Plan for 2026** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The National Energy Administration (国家能源局) released the Energy Field Energy Saving and Carbon Reduction Action Plan (2026–2028), according to the administration. [People's Daily, 2026-07-11](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0711/c1004-40758321.html).
 - By 2028, the proportion of non‑fossil energy consumption is targeted to increase by about **1 percentage point** annually on average, according to the plan. [People's Daily, 2026-07-11](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0711/c1004-40758321.html).
 - The plan aims to increase the proportion of coal‑power capacity meeting the current energy efficiency benchmark by **15 percentage points**, according to the plan. [People's Daily, 2026-07-11](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0711/c1004-40758321.html).
 - The plan proposes piloting **100% new energy** transmission projects, according to the National Energy Administration. [People's Daily, 2026-07-11](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0711/c1004-40758321.html).

- **PBOC tweaks wording in latest meeting, signals policy direction** · covered by 3 outlets
 - The People's Bank of China (中国人民银行) Monetary Policy Committee held its Q2 2026 regular meeting on July 4, according to the committee. [Securities Times](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4007847.html).
 - The meeting adjusted the external environment description from “changes impact deepening” to **“more complex and variable”** and added **“structural differentiation”** to its domestic economic assessment, according to the committee. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-09](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260709/herald/dd043fb78bf3236e1e7622b520862b3e.html).
 - The monetary policy stance remains **moderately loose**, with increased emphasis on forward‑looking, flexible and targeted measures, according to the committee. [China Daily, 2026-07-10](http://caijing.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202607/10/WS6a509b17a310d709c2fbcdd2.html).
 - The committee stated the need to “strengthen the integrated effect of incremental and existing policies” and “maintain ample liquidity”, according to the committee. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-09](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260709/herald/dd043fb78bf3236e1e7622b520862b3e.html).

- **9 Chinese ministries issue 20 measures to boost retail innovation, support qualified firms for IPOs** · covered by 3 outlets
 - The Ministry of Commerce (商务部) and eight other departments issued an opinion proposing **20 policy measures** to accelerate retail innovation and support qualified **new‑quality retail enterprises** in going public, according to the nine departments. [economy.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-09](http://economy.caijing.com.cn/20260709/5169878.shtml).
 - Qualified retail entities are supported to issue asset‑backed securities and commercial real‑estate investment trusts, according to the nine departments. [Securities Times](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4009940.html).
 - The opinion first proposed to explore the establishment of a **commercial facility saturation assessment mechanism** as a market barometer, according to Li Jialu, Director of the Ministry of Commerce’s Department of Circulation Development. [Securities Times](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4009940.html).

- **China financial regulator to include three types of violations in serious dishonesty list** · covered by 4 outlets
 - The National Financial Regulatory Administration (国家金融监管总局) released rules specifying three types of violations that will be included in the list of seriously dishonest entities, according to the administration. [Caixin, 2026-07-10](http://finance.caixin.com/2026-07-10/102462910.html).
 - The three types include administrative penalties such as revocation of business licenses, cancellation or revocation of lifelong qualifications, and lifetime ban from banking or insurance, according to the administration. [Caixin, 2026-07-10](http://finance.caixin.com/2026-07-10/102462910.html).
 - The list takes effect from **October 2026** and the duration on the list is **three years**, according to the administration. [Caixin, 2026-07-10](http://finance.caixin.com/2026-07-10/102462910.html).
 - Entities can apply for early removal after one year if they meet three conditions, according to the National Financial Regulatory Administration. [Securities Times](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4012434.html).

- **China releases 15th Five-Year Carbon Peak Action Plan, targets 25% non-fossil energy consumption by 2030** · covered by 3 outlets
 - The State Council (国务院) released the 15th Five‑Year Carbon Peak Action Plan, according to the plan. [economy.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-10](http://economy.caijing.com.cn/20260710/5170140.shtml).
 - By 2030, China’s unit GDP CO₂ emissions will be **17%** lower than 2025 levels, according to the plan. [People's Daily, 2026-07-09](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0709/c1004-40757097.html).
 - Non‑fossil energy consumption will account for **25%** of total energy use by 2030, according to the plan. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-09](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260709/6315566.shtml).
 - Total installed wind and solar power capacity will reach at least **2.8 billion kW** by 2030, according to the plan. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-09](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260709/6315566.shtml).

### Energy
- **China's National Energy Administration urges faster breakthroughs in supercritical CO₂ power generation, CCUS** · covered by 3 outlets
 - The National Energy Administration (国家能源局) issued the Energy‑Saving and Carbon‑Reducing Action Plan (2026–2028) calling for accelerating breakthroughs in key technologies such as **supercritical CO₂ power generation** and **carbon capture, utilization, and storage (CCUS)**, according to the administration. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-10](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-10/4466546.html).
 - The plan also targets breakthroughs in flexible and efficient wind‑solar hydrogen production and large‑scale safe hydrogen storage technologies, according to the National Energy Administration. [Securities Times, 2026-07-10](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4010949.html).
 - CCUS retrofit of coal‑power units is required to reduce carbon emissions per kWh by about **10%**, according to the plan. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-10](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260710/herald/7f7c407b783c76c7d1f80b3c03192634.html).

 - Ringpu Bio (瑞普生物; a Chinese veterinary pharmaceutical company) and its subsidiary Huanan Bio won the second prize of the National Science and Technology Progress Award for a joint project on avian influenza vaccine, according to the National Science and Technology Award. [Securities Times, 2026-07-09](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4009259.html).

 - Ringpu Bio has launched several key products including H5/H7 tetravalent vaccines, widely applied domestically and exported to Belt and Road countries, according to the company. [Securities Times, 2026-07-09](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4009259.html).
