### Company
- **ChangXin Memory Technologies launches largest-ever STAR Market IPO subscription** · covered by 3 outlets
 - ChangXin Memory Technologies (长鑫科技; China's only DRAM mass-producer, ranking fourth globally with about 8% market share) priced its IPO at **8.66 yuan** per share, issuing **6.688 billion shares** (10% of post-issuance total) to raise about **57.919 billion yuan**, or up to **66.607 billion yuan** if the over-allotment option is fully exercised, making it the largest IPO in STAR Market history, surpassing SMIC's **53.2 billion yuan** record, according to CXMT. [stock.hexun.com, 2026-07-16](http://stock.hexun.com/2026-07-16/224642218.html).
 - The company turned profitable in 2025 with net profit of **1.875 billion yuan**; in Q1 2026 net profit surged to **24.762 billion yuan** on revenue of **50.8 billion yuan**, and it forecasts H1 2026 revenue of **110–120 billion yuan** and net profit of **50–57 billion yuan**, according to ChangXin Memory. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-16](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260716/herald/698a2958f3169a61bf8f61620371dea6.html).
 - Strategic investors include **Montage Technology, Xi'an Yicai, Transsion, and Xiaomi**; the issue price-to-earnings ratio is **308.92 times**, and the subscription code is **787825**, with a maximum single-account subscription of **3.349 million shares**, according to CXMT. [Securities Times, 2026-07-16](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4022002.html).
 - Chairman Zhu Yiming pledged not to sell his shares for ten years after listing and will contribute **768 million shares** for employee equity incentives, according to CXMT. [Jiemian, 2026-07-16](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14728012.html).

### Macro
- **China's H1 new RMB loans reach 10.72 trillion yuan as monetary policy supports real economy** · covered by 3 outlets
 - In the first half of 2026, China's RMB loans increased by **10.72 trillion yuan**, while outstanding social financing at end-June stood at **462.06 trillion yuan**, up **7.4%** year-on-year, according to the People's Bank of China. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-16](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260716/herald/9485e3b1c2d327c2d24afac13cd31fb4.html).
 - Broad money supply (M2) at end-June was **356.71 trillion yuan**, up **8%** year-on-year, and in the first half household loans decreased by **366.8 billion yuan** while corporate loans increased by **11.13 trillion yuan**, according to the PBOC. [People's Daily, 2026-07-15](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0715/c1004-40761322.html).
 - In H1 2026, net corporate bond financing was **2.07 trillion yuan**, an increase of **916.7 billion yuan** year-on-year, and net financing of government bonds was **6.44 trillion yuan**, according to the PBOC. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-15](http://finance.sina.com.cn/china/2026-07-15/doc-inihwmih1190226.shtml).

- **PBOC H1 financial data: corporate direct financing share rises, credit slows but improves quality** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The share of direct financing (corporate bonds and equity) in the total social financing increment reached **11.3%** in H1, up **5.6 percentage points** year-on-year, while new RMB loans in June were **1.61 trillion yuan**, down **630 billion yuan** year-on-year, according to PBOC statistics department director Yan Xiandong. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-16](http://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-07-16/doc-inihxnus4590902.shtml).
 - Non-financial domestic stock financing in H1 was **293.3 billion yuan**, up **122.4 billion yuan** year-on-year, and currency in circulation (M0) at end-June was **14.74 trillion yuan**, up **11.8%** year-on-year, according to the PBOC. [Yicai, 2026-07-16](http://www.yicai.com/news/103276788.html).

### Policy
- **PBOC reaffirms moderately loose monetary policy, vows stronger counter-cyclical adjustment** · covered by 3 outlets
 - The People's Bank of China will continue to implement moderately loose monetary policy and strengthen counter-cyclical and cross-cyclical adjustments to support the economy, according to PBOC Deputy Governor Zou Lan. [economy.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-16](http://economy.caijing.com.cn/20260716/5171317.shtml).
 - As of end-June, social financing stock reached **462.06 trillion yuan**, up **7.4%** year-on-year, and broad money M2 stood at **356.71 trillion yuan**, up **8%**, according to the PBOC. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-16](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-16/doc-inihxxkk7697505.shtml).
 - The central bank will adjust the strength, pace, and timing of monetary policy based on domestic and international economic and financial conditions, aiming to expand domestic demand and consolidate the positive momentum of the economy, Zou Lan added. [www.gelonghui.com, 2026-07-15](http://www.gelonghui.com/live/2554124).

- **China to crack down on 'involution' competition to improve market environment** · covered by 1 outlet
 - President Xi Jinping emphasized deeply rectifying 'involution' competition (self-defeating low-price, homogeneous competition) to create a good market ecology, while the 2026 Government Work Report proposed strengthening anti-monopoly and anti-unfair-competition efforts through capacity regulation, standard guidance, price enforcement, and quality supervision, and called for accelerating revision of the Price Law and E-commerce Law, according to the People's Daily. [People's Daily, 2026-07-17](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0717/c1004-40762263.html).

- **2026 central fiscal support list for inclusive finance demonstration zones released** · covered by 1 outlet
 - The Ministry of Finance released the list of 2026 central financial support for inclusive finance development demonstration zones, requiring services to focus on micro, small and medium enterprises and private firms, with support for first-loan, credit-loan, and medium- to long-term lending, and award funds directed to loan interest subsidies, financing guarantees, risk compensation, and capital replenishment, according to the Ministry. [People's Daily, 2026-07-17](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0717/c1004-40762265.html).
