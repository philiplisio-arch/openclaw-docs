### Trade
- **Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei region posts record H1 import/export volume** · covered by 2 outlets
 - In June alone, exports in the region reached **1,401.3 billion yuan**, an increase of **14.2%**, breaking through the 140 billion yuan mark for the first time in a single month, according to **Beijing Customs**. [People's Daily, 2026-07-20](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0720/c1004-40763788.html).
 - By region, Beijing's import and export value grew **16.5%**, Tianjin's by **2.4%**, and Hebei's by **33.2%**, with Beijing accounting for **69.3%** of the total, according to Beijing Customs. [People's Daily, 2026-07-20](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0720/c1004-40763788.html).
 - Private enterprises' import and export value reached **711.67 billion yuan**, up **21.9%**, 4.9 percentage points higher than the national average for private enterprises. [People's Daily, 2026-07-20](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0720/c1004-40763788.html).
 - Exports of mechanical and electrical products totaled **458.32 billion yuan**, up **17.4%**, accounting for 59.5% of total exports; among them, automobile exports rose **29.3%**, integrated circuits **82.9%**, and lithium-ion batteries **145.5%**, according to Beijing Customs. [People's Daily, 2026-07-20](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0720/c1004-40763788.html).
 - Shanghai's total goods import and export value reached **2.55 trillion yuan** in the first half of this year, up **18.6%** year-on-year, with exports up **20.1%** and imports up **17.4%**, according to the **Shanghai Statistics Bureau**. [Yicai](http://www.yicai.com/news/103282578.html).
 - High-tech product imports and exports grew **21.6%** year-on-year in the first half, and exports of the 'new three items' doubled, according to the Shanghai Statistics Bureau. [Yicai](http://www.yicai.com/news/103282578.html).
