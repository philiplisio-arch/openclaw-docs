## Top Stories

### Trade

- **China's H1 foreign trade hits record 25 trillion yuan, up 16.9%** · covered by 1 outlet
 - China's total imports and exports of goods trade increased by **16.9%** year-on-year in the first half of the year, according to the General Administration of Customs. [Caijing, 2026-07-14](http://economy.caijing.com.cn/20260714/5170738.shtml).
 - Exports reached **14.73 trillion yuan**, up 13.4% year-on-year; imports reached **10.74 trillion yuan**, up 22.1%. [Caijing, 2026-07-14](http://economy.caijing.com.cn/20260714/5170738.shtml).
 - In June, total imports and exports were **4.78 trillion yuan**, a year-on-year increase of **24.2%**. [Caijing, 2026-07-14](http://economy.caijing.com.cn/20260714/5170738.shtml).
 - Exports of electromechanical products reached **9.36 trillion yuan**, up 20.1%, accounting for **63.5%** of total exports. [Caijing, 2026-07-14](http://economy.caijing.com.cn/20260714/5170738.shtml).

- **China's H1 computing hardware trade surges 56.6%** · covered by 3 outlets
 - Imports and exports of computing hardware such as electronic components and computer parts reached **5.13 trillion yuan**, up **56.6%** year-on-year, according to the General Administration of Customs. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260714/6316116.shtml).
 - **267,000** foreign trade enterprises expanded business to new countries and regions, with their total import and export value rising **22.6%** and accounting for nearly **70%** of total trade. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260714/6316116.shtml).
 - Exports of the 'new three items' (electric vehicles, lithium batteries, solar cells), servers and smart terminals grew by **30% to 70%**. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-07-14/doc-inihtrnn5385949.shtml).
 - China has implemented zero-tariff policies for **63** countries. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260714/6316116.shtml).

### Company

- **Multiple firms announce H1 results** · covered by 2 outlets
 - China Life (中国人寿; a Chinese insurance giant) forecasts H1 net profit attributable to parent to increase by approximately **215% to 235%** year-on-year. [life.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://life.caijing.com.cn/20260714/5170996.shtml).
 - Qujiang Cultural Tourism (a Chinese tourism operator) expects H1 net profit attributable to parent to turn profitable at approximately **6.6 million yuan**, attributed to strengthened cost control. [life.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://life.caijing.com.cn/20260714/5170996.shtml).
 - Tongxing Technology (同兴科技; a Chinese technology firm) forecasts H1 net profit of **17 to 20 million yuan**, a year-on-year decline of **61.89% to 67.61%**, citing weaker downstream demand and delayed deliveries. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-14](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-14/4472556.html).

- **LimX Dynamics completes pre-IPO fundraising** · covered by 2 outlets
 - LimX Dynamics (逐际动力; a Chinese humanoid-robotics firm) completed a Pre-IPO round with nearly **$200 million** raised, achieving a post-money valuation of **15 billion yuan**. [tech.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://tech.caijing.com.cn/20260714/5170793.shtml).
 - Investors include IDG Capital, Lens Technology, G.G.G. Group, Redstone VC, Huashan Capital, Hefei Binhu Development Group, and Stone Venture from the UAE. [tech.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://tech.caijing.com.cn/20260714/5170793.shtml).
 - LimX Dynamics has raised a total of **$400 million** in the past six months and launched the full-size interactive humanoid robot LimX Luna (160 cm tall, **27** degrees of freedom). [36Kr, 2026-07-14](http://36kr.com/p/3893976502287618).

- **Tianji Stock faces ST designation over 2023 annual report fraud** · covered by 2 outlets
 - Tianji Stock (天际股份; a Chinese chemical firm) will be suspended on July 14 and renamed to **'ST Tianji'** after resumption on July 15, according to the company. [stock.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://stock.caijing.com.cn/20260714/5170759.shtml).
 - The Guangdong Securities Regulatory Bureau plans to fine Tianji Stock and four executives a total of **5.55 million yuan** for false records in the 2023 annual report, including overstating revenue by approximately **13.6549 million yuan**, cost by **6.8054 million yuan**, and total profit by **6.8495 million yuan**. [stock.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://stock.caijing.com.cn/20260714/5170759.shtml).
 - Tianji Stock reported net profit of **197 million yuan** for Q1 2026, implying a quarter-over-quarter decline of **68.02% to 88.32%** for Q2. [stock.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://stock.caijing.com.cn/20260714/5170759.shtml).

- **JinkoSolar co-founder Chen Kangping steps down from management** · covered by 1 outlet
 - Chen Kangping (陈康平), one of the actual controllers of JinkoSolar (晶科能源; a Chinese solar module maker), resigned from all positions on **July 10, 2026**, after serving as vice chairman since March. [Economic Observer, 2026-07-15](http://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0715/958115.shtml).
 - Chen, as the brother-in-law of chairman Li Xiande, together with the Li brothers, indirectly controls over **55%** of the company's shares. [Economic Observer, 2026-07-15](http://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0715/958115.shtml).
 - New management set a three-year strategy aiming to achieve single-quarter profitability in the second half of 2026. [Economic Observer, 2026-07-15](http://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0715/958115.shtml).

- **Jiangsu Bank appoints new chairman, Gao Zengyin as president** · covered by 2 outlets
 - The Jiangsu Financial Regulatory Bureau approved Yuan Jun (袁军) as chairman of Jiangsu Bank (江苏银行; a Chinese commercial bank). [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260714/6316121.shtml).
 - Yuan Jun resigned as president and chief compliance officer due to position change, and the board appointed Gao Zengyin (高增银) as president, subject to regulatory approval. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-14](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260714/6316121.shtml).
 - Gao Zengyin, born in 1977, holds a master's degree in economics and previously held senior positions at China Construction Bank and Jiangsu Bank. [finance.caijing.com.cn, 2026-07-13](http://finance.caijing.com.cn/20260713/5170679.shtml).

- **Xinhua Department Store shares plunge after controlling shareholder fails to sell stake** · covered by 1 outlet
 - Xinhua Department Store (新华百货; a Chinese retailer) announced termination of a share transfer agreement on **July 12, 2024** (original agreement signed on January 13, 2024, for **22.5631 million shares** at **13 yuan per share**, totaling about **293 million yuan**). [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-13](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260713/herald/2c448998fad2e016c0291d724bd380f2.html).
 - On **July 10, 2024**, the stock closed at **9.51 yuan per share**, nearly erasing gains for the year. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-13](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260713/herald/2c448998fad2e016c0291d724bd380f2.html).

- **Changxin Technology sets IPO price at 8.66 yuan, subscription opens July 16** · covered by 2 outlets
 - Changxin Technology (长鑫科技; a Chinese DRAM maker) set its IPO price at **8.66 yuan per share**, with a subscription date of **July 16, 2026**. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-14](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-14/4472520.html).
 - The IPO consists of **668,808,861 shares** (about **10%** of post-IPO total shares). [National Business Daily, 2026-07-14](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-14/4472520.html).
 - The P/E ratio based on 2025 non-recurring diluted earnings is **308.92 times**, higher than the industry average of **76.32 times**. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-14](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-14/4472520.html).
 - CXMT's market valuation after listing is **579.2 billion yuan**, and in Q1 2026 it achieved revenue of **50.8 billion yuan** and net profit of **26.3 billion yuan**. [Yicai, 2026-07-14](http://www.yicai.com/news/103274921.html).

### Policy

- **China shifts focus to 'goods+services' consumption metric** · covered by 1 outlet
 - In June, the National Bureau of Statistics released the new 'social consumption goods and services retail sales total' indicator, which grew **2.8%** year-on-year from January to May. [People's Daily, 2026-07-15](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0715/c1004-40760730.html).
 - From January to May, service retail sales grew **5.4%** year-on-year, significantly higher than the **1.2%** growth of goods retail sales. [People's Daily, 2026-07-15](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0715/c1004-40760730.html).
 - In 2025, per capita service consumption expenditure accounted for **46.1%** of total consumption expenditure. [People's Daily, 2026-07-15](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0715/c1004-40760730.html).

- **China warns against insurance travel scams violating consumer rights** · covered by 3 outlets
 - The Ministry of Culture and Tourism and the National Financial Regulatory Administration issued a risk alert on **July 14, 2026**, warning about deceptive marketing using 'insurance client tourism' to lure consumers into unregulated tour groups. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-14](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260714/herald/29d6df338557da481f1edb6e832299c4.html).
 - During trips, consumers may be forced into shopping through means such as moral blackmail, verbal threats, detention, and withholding room keys. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-14](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260714/herald/29d6df338557da481f1edb6e832299c4.html).
 - Consumers are advised to verify the authenticity of insurance-related travel activities through official insurer channels. [Securities Times, 2026-07-14](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4018472.html).

- **China to boost yacht consumption with improved port infrastructure** · covered by 1 outlet
 - Minister of Transport Liu Wei (刘伟) emphasized strengthening infrastructure for piers and berths to promote mass consumption of yachts, alongside standardizing yacht safety management. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-14](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260714/herald/23370e02539830374e638fde012effe2.html).

- **Shandong's Nishan area leverages Confucius heritage for economic development** · covered by 1 outlet
 - The Nishan World Civilization Forum and China International Confucius Culture Festival are held in Qufu. [People's Daily, 2026-07-15](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0715/c1004-40760729.html).
 - Jining was selected as one of China's top 100 tourism cities in 2025, and the topic 'Cultural China Tour, Welcome to Jining' has accumulated over **2 billion views** on overseas social media. [People's Daily, 2026-07-15](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0715/c1004-40760729.html).

- **China issues consumer guidance on flight overbooking rights** · covered by 1 outlet
 - A survey by consumer associations in Beijing, Tianjin, and Hebei found that **84.85%** of passengers have heard of airline ticket overbooking, but **67.12%** cannot fully grasp overbooking rules. [People's Daily, 2026-07-15](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0715/c1004-40760732.html).
 - Only **26.96%** of passengers clearly know their rights after overbooking, and **71.61%** report that the overbooking risk warning text is too small or hidden. [People's Daily, 2026-07-15](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0715/c1004-40760732.html).
