### Policy
- **PBOC announces measures to boost HK stock listings, Bond Connect, forex reserves allocation** · covered by 3 outlets
 - The PBOC (中国人民银行; China’s central bank) will expand the RMB business funding arrangement for Hong Kong from **200 billion yuan** to **500 billion yuan** and extend the tenor up to **3 years**, according to the PBOC. [36Kr, 2026-07-08](http://36kr.com/p/3886416284790787).

[finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-08](http://finance.sina.com.cn/money/bank/bank_hydt/2026-07-08/doc-inifzwqf1475388.shtml).
