## Top Stories

### Macro
- **China June manufacturing PMI comes in at 50.3%** · covered by 2 outlets
 - Manufacturing PMI was **50.3%** in June, up 0.3 percentage points from the previous month and remaining at or above 50% for four consecutive months, according to the National Bureau of Statistics Service Industry Survey Center and the China Federation of Logistics & Purchasing. [People's Daily, 2026-07-07](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0707/c1004-40754620.html).
 - The second-quarter manufacturing PMI averaged **50.2%**, returning to expansion after five consecutive quarters of contraction. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-07](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607073795562347.html).
 - New orders index rose to **51.2%** in June (up 1.3 pp), while new export orders returned to expansion at **50.1%** (up 1.5 pp). [People's Daily, 2026-07-07](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0707/c1004-40754620.html).
 - Equipment manufacturing PMI reached **52.5%** (up 0.4 pp) and high-tech manufacturing PMI hit **53.5%** (up 0.6 pp). [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-07](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607073795562347.html).

### Policy
- **China adjusts vehicle tax breaks for energy-saving and NEVs** · covered by 1 outlet
 - From January 1, 2027, the policy of halving vehicle and vessel tax for energy-saving vehicles and exempting certain new energy commercial vehicles will be cancelled, according to the Ministry of Finance, State Administration of Taxation, and Ministry of Industry and Information Technology. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-06](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260706/6314880.shtml).
 - The tax-reduction policy had been in place since **2012**; the adjustment is intended to promote tax fairness and enhance the regulatory role of taxation on income distribution, per the three ministries. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-06](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260706/6314880.shtml).
 - In 2025, China sold **16.49 million** new energy vehicles, with domestic sales share exceeding 50%; pure electric passenger cars and fuel cell passenger cars remain outside the scope of vehicle and vessel tax. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-06](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260706/6314880.shtml).

### Company
- **Unitree Technology IPO registration approved** · covered by 2 outlets
 - Unitree Technology's (宇树科技; a Chinese humanoid-robot developer) IPO registration became effective on July 6, 2026, according to the Shanghai Stock Exchange; the CSRC approved the STAR Market registration on July 2. [m.21jingji.com, 2026-07-06](http://m.21jingji.com/article/20260706/herald/af79281e0e2436cb81068df20d594a38.html).
 - Unitree's revenue grew from **159 million yuan** in 2023 to **1.699 billion yuan** in 2025 (CAGR 226.78%), and gross margin increased from 44.22% to 60.13%, per the prospectus. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-06](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607063795238462.html).
 - Unitree plans to raise **4.202 billion yuan** in the IPO for intelligent robot model R&D projects, issuing at least 40.446 million new shares (no less than 10% of post-IPO total shares). [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-06](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607063795238462.html).
- **Jincheng Pharmaceutical expects H1 net profit to surge up to 223%** · covered by 3 outlets
 - Jincheng Pharmaceutical (金城医药; a Chinese pharmaceutical maker) expects H1 2026 net profit of **110–140 million yuan**, up 153.55%–222.71% YoY, driven by higher sales volume and capacity utilization in the intermediate segment plus higher non-recurring gains. [www.gelonghui.com, 2026-07-06](http://www.gelonghui.com/live/2538550).
 - Binhua Co., Ltd. (滨化股份; a Chinese chemical company) expects net profit of **344 million yuan** for H1 2026, up 208.25% YoY, attributed to stable plant operations and higher prices of propylene oxide, allyl chloride, and propylene amid global chemical raw material price hikes. [Jiemian, 2026-07-06](http://www.jiemian.com/article/14711029.html).
 - Yanjing Beer (燕京啤酒; a Chinese brewer) expects net profit of **1.379–1.489 billion yuan** for H1 2026, up 25%–35% YoY, with Q2 net profit up 320%–362% sequentially from Q1. [finance.eastmoney.com, 2026-07-06](http://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607063795254063.html).
- **Exchange suspends trading for investors after Zhonggong Education stock hits limit-up** · covered by 2 outlets
 - Zhonggong Education (中公教育; a Chinese vocational training provider) hit its daily limit up of **10%** on July 6, closing at **2.09 yuan** per share (market cap 12.9 billion yuan); the stock has hit two daily limits in four trading days. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-06](http://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-07-06/doc-inifwsct1853209.shtml).
 - The Shenzhen Stock Exchange reported it is closely monitoring trading and took regulatory measures including suspending trading for certain investors. [Securities Times, 2026-07-06](http://www.stcn.com/article/detail/4000919.html).
 - The controlling shareholder and its concerted parties have a high proportion of pledged and frozen shares, with heavy debt burden and tight capital chain; management said it will prioritize debt restructuring. [finance.sina.com.cn, 2026-07-06](http://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-07-06/doc-inifwsct1853209.shtml).

### Trade
- **China's shipbuilding industry chain expands, data shows** · covered by 1 outlet
 - During the 14th Five-Year Plan period, China's shipbuilding industry received **64.2%** of global new ship orders, and its market share has ranked first worldwide for 16 consecutive years. [People's Daily, 2026-07-07](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0707/c1004-40754587.html).
 - In December 2025, the world's first methanol dual-fuel intelligent VLCC "Kai Tuo" was delivered by Dalian Shipbuilding, with CO2, SOx, and PM emissions reduced by over **90%**. [People's Daily, 2026-07-07](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0707/c1004-40754587.html).
 - In 2025, Nantong's shipbuilding and marine engineering industry output exceeded **220 billion yuan**, accounting for 1/8 of national shipbuilding output. [People's Daily, 2026-07-07](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0707/c1004-40754587.html).

### Tech
- **China's electronics manufacturing output grows 14.6% in Jan-May** · covered by 3 outlets
 - Value-added of China's electronic information manufacturing industry increased by **14.6%** year-on-year in the first five months of 2026; smartphone production reached **477 million units** (up 3.3%), and integrated circuit production reached **228.6 billion units** (up 25.4%), according to the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology. [People's Daily, 2026-07-06](http://finance.people.com.cn/n1/2026/0706/c1004-40753849.html).
 - The industry achieved total profit of **422 billion yuan** in the first five months, up 104% year-on-year; fixed asset investment rose 6.7% YoY. [finance.china.com.cn, 2026-07-05](http://finance.china.com.cn/news/20260705/6314837.shtml).
 - Operating revenue reached **7.52 trillion yuan**, up 17.1% year-on-year, and export delivery value grew 6.1%. [National Business Daily, 2026-07-05](http://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-07-05/4452511.html).
