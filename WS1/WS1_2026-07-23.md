- **Auto Analyst: Profit per 200,000 Yuan Car Only 3,000 Yuan; Cost Restructuring Needed** · covered by 2 outlets
 - From January to May, the profit margin of China's auto industry was only **3.4%**, and the profit margin for automobile manufacturing was only **1.5%**, a historic low, according to the China Passenger Car Association (乘联分会). [36Kr, 2026-07-23](http://36kr.com/p/3906491481249153).
 - Fuel vehicle prices dropped by an average of **14.9%**, and new energy vehicle prices dropped by about **12%** from January to June, according to Cui Dongshu (崔东树), Secretary-General of the China Passenger Car Association. [36Kr, 2026-07-23](http://36kr.com/p/3906491481249153).
 - In June, retail sales of fuel passenger cars fell by nearly **40%** year-on-year, while the penetration rate of new energy passenger vehicles reached **62.8%**, according to the China Passenger Car Association. [36Kr, 2026-07-23](http://36kr.com/p/3906491481249153).
